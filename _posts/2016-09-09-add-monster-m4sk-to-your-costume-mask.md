[From the Adafruit Learn System](https://learn.adafruit.com/add-monster-m4sk-to-your-costume-mask/overview) – The Adafruit MONSTER M4SK’s screens are perfect for adding animated eyeballs to your costume mask. With some simple additions made from craft supplies, we can make the M4SK comfortable to wear and allow you to see your surroundings through the costume mask’s eye holes.
