![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Airtel-logo.jpg) Shortly after Vodafone Idea announced their new prepaid plans, Bharati Airtel has now released their new prepaid tariff. The new plans represent a hike in tariff between 50 paise per day to Rs 2.85 per day. Similar to Jio and Vodafone Idea, Fair Usage Policy applies on unlimited calling from Airtel to other networks, and users will be charged 6 paise/min beyond FUP. Users won't be charged 6 paise/min for Airtel to Airtel calls. Here is the list of new prepaid plans from Airtel Rs. 19 - Unlimited calling, 150 MB data, 100 SMS, 2 days validity Rs. 49 - Rs. 38.52 talk time, 100 MB data, 28 days validity Rs. 79 - Rs. 63.95 talk time, 200 MB data, 28 days validity Unlimited plans with 28 days validity Rs. 148 - Unlimited calling, 2 GB data, 300 SMS Rs. 248 - Unlimited calling, 1.5 GB/day, 100 SMS/day Rs. 298 - Unlimited calling, 2GB/day, 100 SMS/day Unlimited plans with 84 days validity Rs. 598 - Unlimited calling, 1.5 GB/day, 100 SMS/day Rs. 698 - Unlimited calling, 2 GB/day, 100 SMS/day Unlimited plans with 365 days (1 Year) validity Rs. 1498 -Unlimited calling, 24 GB, 3600 SMS Rs. 2398 - Unlimited calling, 1.5 GB/day, 100 SMS/day Rs. ...
