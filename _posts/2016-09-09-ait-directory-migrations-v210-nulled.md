### One Click Content Migration Directory Product

Migrate content of your Directory or Business Finder based portals with ease to City Guide theme. This plugin prevents your from copying items, categories, locations, etc. manually.

**Available Plugin Features**

*   Migrate your old data from Directory and Business finder themes to our new directory themes
*   You can migrate Items, Item Categories, Item Locations and Reviews (Item Reviews must be already installed)
*   Migrate your existing users to new packages
*   WPML compatibility to migrate from your WPML translated site (AIT Langauges plugin must be already installed)
*   In case something went wrong, the log file of whole process will be available throughout migration

**Compatible themes:** BusinessFinder+ City Guide Directory+ Event Guide FoodGuide
