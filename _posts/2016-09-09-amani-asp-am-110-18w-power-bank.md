![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/Amani-18W-power-bank-1024x605.jpg) AMANI, a mobile accessories brand, has launched the ASP-AM 110, which is a palm sized 10000 mAh power bank capable of delivering up to 18W charging. It has dual USB outputs to allow two phones to be charged at the same time and comes in black and white colours. The power bank is built with high quality ABS plastic and charges via the included micro USB cable. The battery capacity is rated at 10000mAh and its levels are shown with a 4 level indicator. It can output between 5.1 V/2.4 A or 9 V/2 A depending on the connected device. The AMANI ASP-AM 110 is available for purchase for a price of Rs. 1199 on Amanimart.com Commenting on the launch of the power bank, Tarun Bhutani, Managing Director at AMANI, said: We at AMANI are always in the verge of developing quality products for our customers. Our products are designed and developed addressing the customers' needs and what more they are looking for. We are confident that the 10,000 mAH AMANI ASP-AM 110palm sized power bank, which is one of our promising quality products, will be loved by our customers. We are all geared up to introduce more unique and quality products in 2020 ...
