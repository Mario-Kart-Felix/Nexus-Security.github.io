![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/Amazfit-Verge-1024x642.jpg) Huami Corporation, the makers of the Amazfit Verge has released an update to the smartwatch in India that enable two new functionalities; Amazon Alexa Voice-Assistant integration and the ability for iOS users to make and receive calls using the device's in-built microphone and speakers. With the Alexa integration built in now, users can now speak to Amazfit Verge smartwatch and get the following: Flash Briefing — Set up and enable Alexa skills of your favorite news providers such NDTV or Cricinfo to personalize daily “Flash Briefing”. Sports Update — Follow your favourite cricket team and track the latest cricket scores. Alarm — Set a reminder or wake-up alarm. To-do list — Manage your schedule and to-do list. Q and A — With Alexa built-in, users can get answers to common questions. The smartwatch requires the installation of the Amazfit Verge smartwatch app, “Amazfit Watch” which is available for free on iOS and Android. The Amazfit Verge is now available from Amazon.in for Rs. 10,999.
