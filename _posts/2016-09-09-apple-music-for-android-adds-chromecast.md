![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Apple-Music-Chromecast-support.jpg) Apple added a new Dark mode on Apple Music Beta for Android along with synchronized lyrics with music feature earlier this month. Now, with the Stable update on Apple Music for Android, Apple is adding Dark mode, Chromecast support, and 1,00,000 radio stations. Apple Music app will now show the 'Cast' icon when it detects a Chromecast or a Chromecast built-in device. Apple has also added 1,00,000 radio stations with this update and is sourced from Radio.com, TuneIn, and iHeartRadio which can be found by simply searching the channel name or its frequency. The last addition is the new Dark mode that is designed for low-light environments. Changelog Now stream your favourite music to your TV or connected speakers with Chromecast. With this update you can also: - Listen to your favourite music with perfectly timed lyrics. - Experience a new dark mode designed for low-light environments that’s easy on your eyes. - Access live radio stations from around the world.
