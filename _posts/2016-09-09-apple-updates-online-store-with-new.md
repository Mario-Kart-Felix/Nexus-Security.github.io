---
title: 'Apple Updates Online Store With New Watch Bands, Silicone Cases for iPhone 11 Pro, and Sony''s DualShock 4'
date: 2019-11-13T17:10:00+01:00
draft: false
---

Apple today updated its online store with a [new collection of Apple Watch bands](https://www.apple.com/shop/watch/bands), iPhone cases, and the Sony DualShock 4 controller. For [Apple Watch](https://www.macrumors.com/roundup/apple-watch/) bands, this includes three new Sport Bands: Pomegranate, Beryl, and Khaki.  
  
  
  
  
  
![](https://cdn.macrumors.com/article-new/2019/11/new-bands-nov-2019.jpg)  
  
  
There's only one new Sport Loop, and it's for the (Product)Red initiative. This is the new dual-color style Sport Loop, with two shades of red along the length of the band.  
  
  
  
  
  
![](https://cdn.macrumors.com/article-new/2019/11/product-red-sport-loop-2019.jpg)  
  
  
There are also two new Hermès bands: Noir/Brique/Étain Swift Leather Double Tour and Noir/Brique/Étain Swift Leather Single Tour.  
  
  
  
  
  
![](https://cdn.macrumors.com/article-new/2019/11/new-hermes-nov-2019.jpg)  
  
  
For iPhone, you can get the new Beryl and Pomegranate colors in a [Silicone Case](https://www.apple.com/shop/accessories/all-accessories/seasonal-colors) for the [iPhone 11 Pro](https://www.macrumors.com/roundup/iphone-11-pro/) or [iPhone 11 Pro Max](https://www.macrumors.com/roundup/iphone-11-pro/).  
  
  
  
  
  
![](https://cdn.macrumors.com/article-new/2019/11/iphone-new-silicone-cases.jpg)  
  
  
[Sony's DualShock 4 controller](https://www.apple.com/shop/product/HNKS2VC/A/sony-playstation-dualshock4-wireless-controller) has also been added to Apple.com at a price of $59.95. In [iOS 13](https://www.macrumors.com/roundup/ios-13/) and [tvOS 13](https://www.macrumors.com/roundup/tvos-13/), you can connect either a DualShock 4 or Xbox Wireless Controller to your Apple device to play compatible games or even control your PS4 remotely [via Sony's Remote Play iOS app](https://www.macrumors.com/2019/06/05/ios-13-iphone-ps4-remote-play/).  
  
  
  
  
  
![](https://cdn.macrumors.com/article-new/2019/06/iOS-13-ps4-dualshock.jpg)  
  
  
Lastly, Otterbox recently launched the [Figura Series Otter + Pop cases](https://www.apple.com/shop/product/HNLF2ZM/A/otterbox-figura-pop-series-case-for-iphone-11) for [iPhone 11](https://www.macrumors.com/roundup/iphone-11/), 11 Pro, and 11 Pro Max. These cases are exclusive to Apple and combine Otterbox's protection with a built-in PopSocket accessory, for $59.95.  
  
  
  
  
  
![](https://cdn.macrumors.com/article-new/2019/11/pop-socket-otterbox.jpg)  
  
  
Apple updated its online store today alongside the release of the 16-inch [MacBook Pro](https://www.macrumors.com/roundup/macbook-pro/), which is now available to order starting at $2,399 in the United States. [Our announcement post](https://www.macrumors.com/2019/11/13/16-inch-macbook-pro-announced-with-up-to-64gb-of-ram-and-8tb-ssd-new-keyboard-louder-speakers-and-more/) has more information on the new ‌MacBook Pro‌.
