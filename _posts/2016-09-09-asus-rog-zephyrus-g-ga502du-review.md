![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/ASUS-ROG-Zephyrus-G-GA502DU_fonearena-6-1024x581.jpg) When ASUS announced the ROG Zephyrus G GA502 earlier in 2019, the laptop made quite a buzz as it was powered by a rare combination of Ryzen 7 3rd Gen mobile CPU's and a 1660Ti Max-Q GPU. Along with this, having a screen with a 120 Hz refresh rate and an interesting price point of Rs. 89990, the laptop could be a very good mid range gaming laptop for consumers. In this review, we check out the laptop to see what compromises ASUS made to achieve this price and why should you consider it for your next gaming laptop.
