> On January 20, 2020 the National Park Service (NPS) will host an “open house” of Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Birth home during the hours of 10:00 a.m. until 4:30 p.m. The following locations (Historic Ebenezer Baptist Church, Visitor Center and Fire Station No.6.) will be open to the general public from 9:00 a.m. until 5:00 p.m.

For more information, please visit the [National Park Service website](https://www.nps.gov/planyourvisit/event-details.htm?id=146D19D2-C8E1-775D-45EFE6A301E27533).
