We’re getting close to adding support for a new STM32F4 board to CircuitPython ([video](https://youtu.be/YFTbBri0rpc)). This is the KittenBot Meowbit, its microbit-like at the bottom but has a display and d-pad for Microsoft Arcade games. And now it even works with CircuitPython! Stay tuned for when the PR is merged, and try it yourself.
