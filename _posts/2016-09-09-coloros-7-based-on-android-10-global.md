![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/ColorOS-7-1.jpg) OPPO today launched the ColorOS 7 for the global markets, at an event in India, as it had promised. It also announced ColorOS 7 roll out details for several phones, and said that it will be based on Android 10. ColorOS 7 introduces a simple design with lighter icons that are easier to interact and lighter in vision. It brings new Soloop video editor, new DocVault apps, Three-fine screenshot, Music Party, AI Beautification 2.0, App Lock, Private Safe, Osense and lots more. You can check out all the features here. Here is the list of OPPO devices that are getting the ColorOS 7 update starting from today in the form of trial version, which is like beta. You can join this from Settings > Software Updates > Setting Icon — Trail Version. You can check out the realme device list here. Phase 1: from today November 26 OPPO Reno OPPO Reno 10x Zoom Phase 2: In December 2019 OPPO Reno 2 OPPO F11 OPPO F11 Pro OPPO F11 Pro Marvel's Avengers Limited Edition Phase 3: In Q1 2020 OPPO Find X OPPO Find X Lamorgini Edition OPPO Find X SuperVOOC Edition OPPO Reno 2F OPPO Reno Z OPPO R17 OPPO R17 Pro OPPO RX17 Pro ...
