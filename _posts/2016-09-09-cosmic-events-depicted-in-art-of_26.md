> A selection of new work by artist [Michael Dandley](https://www.instagram.com/michaeldandley/) (previously featured [here](https://www.booooooom.com/tag/michael-dandley/)). Currently studying for his Masters in Fine Arts in Zurich, Switzerland, Dandley’s third solo exhibition at [Nahcotta](https://nahcotta.com/collections/michael-dandley-high-low-places)depicts a series of cosmic events that appear simultaneously wondrous and cataclysmic.
