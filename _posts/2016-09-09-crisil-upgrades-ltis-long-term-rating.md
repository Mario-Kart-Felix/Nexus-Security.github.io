[CRISIL upgrades LTI’s long-term rating to AAA/Stable](https://varindia.com/news/crisil-upgrades-ltis-longterm-rating-to-aaastable#.XkPMpyI3ADI.blogger): CRISIL, India’s leading credit rating agency, has upgraded Larsen & Toubro Infotech’s (LTI) long-term rating to AAA/Stable from AA+/Positive. Headquartered in Mumbai, LTI is a global technology consulting and digital solutions company with more than 31,000 employees in 30 countries.
