John Deere is widely known for their iconic green and yellow tractors. Now the company is teaming up with Volocoptor to take to the skies!

Via [Tech Crunch](https://techcrunch.com/2019/11/07/volocopter-and-john-deere-team-up-for-a-crop-spraying-autonomous-agricultural-drone/):

> Autonomous drone-based transportation startup [Volocopter](https://crunchbase.com/organization/volocopter) has revealed its first partner for its new VoloDrone industrial and commercial electric vertical take-off and landing craft: [John Deere](https://crunchbase.com/organization/john-deere) . The agricultural and industrial heavy equipment company is working with Volocopter on a VoloDrone-based aerial crop-dusting system.
