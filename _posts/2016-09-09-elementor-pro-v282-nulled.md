![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Samsung-Galaxy-S10-Lite-1024x723.jpg) Samsung introduced the Galaxy S10 Lite and Note10 Lite smartphones yesterday, and the phones will be showcased at the CES 2020 next week. Now Flipkart has confirmed that it will launch the Galaxy S10 Lite smartphone in India soon. Samsung has also enabled notify option for the  S10 Lite and Note10 Lite on its website, so we can expect both the phones as early as this month. To remind you the phone pack a 6.7-inch Full HD+ Super AMOLED Infinity-O Display, in-display fingerprint scanner, 32-megapixel front camera inside the tiny punch-hole and a 4500mAh battery with support for 25w fast charging. The Galaxy Note10 Lite features the signature Bluetooth S Pen with Air Commands. The S10 Lite is powered by Snapdragon 855, while the Note10 Lite sticks to old Exynos 9810 SoC, but it has a 3.5mm audio jack, which the S10 Lite lacks. The S10 Lite packs a 48MP main camera with Super Steady OIS, 12MP ultra-wide and a 5MP macro camera, while the Note10 Lite comes with 12MP main camera with OIS, 12MP ultra-wide and a 12MP Telephoto camera.
