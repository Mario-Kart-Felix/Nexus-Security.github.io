Integrates GetResponse into the ExchangeWP plugin.
--------------------------------------------------

Allow customers to sign up for your email newsletters by adding an opt-in checkbox to the user registration form of your ExchangeWP store.

GetResponse Settings
--------------------

After uploading and activating the GetResponse Add-on, click the settings gear on the ExchangeWP Add-ons page to configure your GetResponse Settings.

Add your **GetResponse API Key** and select which** GetResponse list** you want to add your customer subscribers.

Customize your **GetResponse label** (the message that displays next to the opt-in checkbox on the customer registration form) and select whether to check the GetResponse box by default.
