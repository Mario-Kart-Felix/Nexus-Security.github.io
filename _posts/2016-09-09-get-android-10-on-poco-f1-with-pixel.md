![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/POCO-F1-Android-10-Pixel-Experience_fonearena-1024x655.jpg) Xiaomi’s sub-brand POCO launched the POCO F1 smartphone in India back in August 2018 running MIUI 9.6 Global Stable on top of Android 8.1 (Oreo) and later updated it to Android 9.0 in December 2018. The device runs MIUI 10 based on Android 9.0 as of now, and should get MIUI 11 shortly as per the announced schedule. POCO F1 is confirmed to get Android 1o update but the company has not shared any timeline for Android 10 rollout but POCO F1 has one of the most active developer community and as a result, we already have a few Android 10 based custom ROMs for the device. We tried Android 10 based Pixel Experience on our unit, and here is our post that may help you in deciding whether you should flash it on your unit. Performance and Benchmarks \[gallery size="large" link="file" ids="295942,295943,295944"\] POCO F1 packs Qualcomm Snapdragon 845 which is a beast when it comes to performance, it obtained AnTuTu score of 226832 on Pixel Experience. It scored 2025 points in single-core test and 6275 points in multi-core test in Geekbench 4.
