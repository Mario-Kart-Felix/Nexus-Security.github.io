![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Redmi-Note-5-Pro-Android-10-Pixel-Experience_fonearena-1-1024x661.jpg) Xiaomi Redmi Note 5 Pro was amongst best-selling devices last year, Xiaomi launched it running on Android Nougat, and was updated to Android 8.1 (Oreo) in June 2018. The company rolled out Android 9.0 (Pie) update to the smartphone back in May this year, the device is almost 18 months old and has already received two Android updates. It is almost certain that Xiaomi will not update the Android version on the Redmi Note 5 Pro, but in case you own a Redmi Note 5 Pro and wish to keep your device updated with the latest Android version, you may try a custom ROM. We had earlier tried Android 10 based Arrow OS on our Redmi Note 5 Pro and now, we are back with Android 10 based Pixel Experience custom ROM review. Performance and Benchmarks: \[gallery size="large" link="file" ids="296713,296714,296715"\] It obtained AnTuTu score of 138220 on Pixel Experience. It scored 1326 points in single-core test and 4761 points in multi-core test in Geekbench 4. In Geekbench 5 test, it managed to get a 282 points in single-core test and 1242 points in multi-core test. Software, UI, and Apps: \[gallery size="large" link="file" ids="296712,296718,296719"\] As the name suggests, the Pixel Experience ROM aims towards providing a clean Pixel-like experience on the device.
