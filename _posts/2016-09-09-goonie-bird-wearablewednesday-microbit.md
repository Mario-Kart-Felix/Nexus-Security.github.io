Mike Owen [shared](https://learn.adafruit.com/how-to-send-a-blogtip-to-adafruit/) this excellent project. A wearable glove uses gestures to give the animatronic bird commands. The addition of the Monster M4sk adds a whole extra dimension of expressiveness!

Via [Mike Owen](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCkmLFpKTPK4TuwlWWA00CIA) on [YouTube](https://youtu.be/gdBqlNb886M):

> A “Monster Mashup” using Adafruit’s cool Monster M4sk eyes and a couple of Micro:Bits. It even lay’s golden eggs! (the caramel filled chocolate kind….)  
> Enjoy!
