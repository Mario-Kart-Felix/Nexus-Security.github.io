![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Havit-i37-Wireless-Headset-1024x911.jpg) HAVIT, a consumer electronics brand has announced its first product in India, the Havit i37 wireless headset. The neckband style headset features strong bass performance, Bluetooth 5.0, IPX5 rating and good HiFi Stereo sound performance. The i37 can be controlled via the in-built remote enables the user take/ end calls with its microphone, skip and pause tracks and control volume. There is also voice assistant support with Google and Siri. Being an in-ear design, it creates a strong seal, boosting bass performance. They have an IPX5 sweat and dust resistance rating, making it ideal for workouts and daily usage without the worry of damaging the earphones. Both earbuds also feature magnetic strips that prevent tangling and even automatically turns off when the two earbuds are connected to each other. Coming to battery life, the headset offers 10 hours of continuous listening time, 12 hours back of time talk, 180 hours of standby. It comes in 4 colours namely black, white, blue and red. The HAVIT i37 is available for purchase online on Amazon and Flipkart for a price of Rs. 1169.
