![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/HiFuture-NeckLace-1-1024x768.jpg) HiFuture, an consumer audio manufacturer, has launched their new product, the HiFuture NeckLace in-Ear Wireless Earphones in India. The earphones features an ergonomic neckband, sweatproof design with Bluetooth 5.0 and a long claimed battery life. HiFuture claims to have put a lot of thought into the design of the earphones, with a smooth glide-on design, where no adjustment is required once the user puts in the ear. The neckband is completely made out of silicon and the earphones have magnets that allow it to clip together when not in use. The earphones connect via Bluetooth 5.0 and support Qualcomms AptX and AAC hi-res audio for a clear music experience. The 10mm neodymium magnets provide for an immersive sound experience and HiFuture claims to use in-built motion sensors that work together with advanced acoustic system to provide ultra plus soft bass. We are unclear as to how exactly that works and will require to test that claim in the review. Another highlight feature in the earphones is the presence of Environmental Noise Cancellation technology, thanks to Qualcomm CVC 8.0 which uses the inbuilt mics to efficiently filter out background noise so communication remains crystal clear no matter the environment.
