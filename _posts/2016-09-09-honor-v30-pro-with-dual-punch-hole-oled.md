![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/HONOR-V30-Pro-leak-.jpg) HUAWEI's HONOR brand's President Zhao Ming back in August confirmed that the upcoming HONOR V30 will come with 5G support. Now more details and some images of the V30 and V30 Pro have surfaced. According to the latest leak, both the phones will be powered by Kirin 990 SoC, but the V30 Pro will be a huge upgrade. The HONOR V30 is said to feature an LCD punch-hole screen, pack 60MP triple rear cameras, which could use new Sony IMX686 sensor and a 4000mAh battery with support for 22.5W fast charging. The HONOR V30 Pro on the other hand is said to feature dual in-screen punch-hole 6.5-inch OLED display, which could come with 90Hz refresh rate, powered by Kirin 990 5G SoC, pack 60MP quad rear cameras and a 4200mAh battery with support for 40W super fast charging and 15W wireless fast charging. The HONOR V30 series is said to be released by the end of this month, and the V30 is said to start at 3,000 yuan (US$ 422 / Rs. 30,010 approx.), while the V30 Pro with 5G is rumored to be priced starting at 4,000 yuan (US$ 563/ Rs. 40,020 approx.).
