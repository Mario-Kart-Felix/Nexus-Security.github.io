![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/HTC-Desire-19s.jpg) HTC has just announced its latest Desire 19s smartphone under Desire series and is the successor of the Desire 12s that was launched last year. HTC Desire 19s comes with a waterdrop-styled notch display, triple rear cameras, multi-layered exquisite texture, and more. It flaunts a 6.2-inch 19:9 HD+ display with a resolution of 720 x 1520 pixels, is powered by MediaTek Helio P22 processor paired with 3GB RAM with 32GB internal storage. The glass to the back has a multi-layered exquisite texture pattern and a light-like color transparency. It runs on Android 9 Pie on top of HTC's Sense UI skin. Regarding the optics, it sports triple rear cameras with a combination of 13MP (primary with f/1.85 aperture) +5MP (ultra-wide with f/2.2 aperture) +5MP (depth sensor with f/2.2 aperture). The front camera is a 16MP sensor with an f/2.0 aperture. The connectivity options include Dual Band WiFi, Bluetooth 5.0, NFC, GPS, and also retains the 3.5mm headphone jack. It sports a rear-mounted fingerprint scanner and packs a 3850mAh battery.
