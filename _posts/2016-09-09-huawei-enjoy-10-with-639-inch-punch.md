![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/HUAWEI-Enjoy-10-3.jpg) After the Enjoy 10 Plus last month, HUAWEI has introduced Enjoy 10, a cheaper phone in the Enjoy series in China, as it had promised.  It packs a 6.39-inch HD+ display with an 8-megapixel in-screen camera, is powered by an octa-core Kirin 710F 12nm SoC with up to 6GB RAM and GPU Turbo 3.0, has a 48-megapixel primary camera with AI features and a 2-megapixel camera for portrait shots. It runs EMUI 9.1on top of Android 9 Pie, features rear-mounted fingerprint sensor and is backed up by a 4000mAh (typical) battery.
