![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/HUAWEI-Nova-6-5G.jpg) Update: HUAWEI has posted official renders of the Nova 6 5G that shows the phone's dual punch-hole screen with minimal bezels, and triple rear cameras that includes a telephoto lens with OIS. It has also started pre-bookings for the phone in China. After rumors, HUAWEI has finally confirmed that it will introduce Nova 6 5G, the company's new flagship Nova series phone at an event in China on December 5th, few days after it introduces HUAWEI Mate Pad series later this month. The new teaser confirms dual punch-hole front cameras, and one of the cameras will let you capture 105-degree wide-angle selfies, and a main camera will have selfie super night scene 2.0, and it could use 32-megapixel sensor. The phone will come with 5G SA/NSA dual mode support. TENAA and 3C certified revealed 40W HUAWEI SuperCharge, and 4G and 5G models, powered by Kirin 990 and Kirin 990 5G SoCs. Earlier renders revealed quad rear cameras. We should know more details about the smartphone in the coming weeks before the phone goes official. Source 1, 2, 3
