![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/Infinix-Band-5-1024x710.jpg) Infinix has launched Band 5, the company’s latest fitness tracker and the successor of the Xband 3 which was launched back in June. It has 0.96-inch color screen that displays icons for different functions. It lets you monitor your heart rate and the fitness band is IP67 rated for water resistance against accidental splashes and dust. Infinix Band 5 highlights and features 0.96-inch (160 x 80 pixels) TFT-LCD screen Bluetooth 4.0 to connect to devices running Android 4.4 or later and iOS 9.0 or later Step count, sleep tracking and calorie consumption data Display Time/Date, Alarm Clock, Smartphone Notifications (Call, Calendar, Text Notifications, App Alerts), Sedentary Reminder, Shake to take a picture. PPG Heart Rate Sensor for Heart rate tracking 3-axis accelerometer for activity, exercise and sleep tracking Water resistant (IP67) Up to 7 days of battery life, 22 days of standby The Band 5 comes in Black, Blue and Red colors, is priced at Rs. 1799 and will be available from Flipkart during Big Shopping Days sale from December 1st.
