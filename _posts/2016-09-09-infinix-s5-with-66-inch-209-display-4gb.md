![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Infinix-S5.jpg) Infinix has launched S5, the company's latest smartphone in the S series in India. It has a 6.6-inch HD+ display with a 32-megapixel in-screen camera, 90.5% screen-to-body ratio, is powered by Octa-Core MediaTek Helio P22 12nm SoC with 4GB of RAM, runs Android 9.0 (Pie) with XOS 5.5, has a 16-megapixel rear camera with quad LED flash, along with a 5-megapixel 114-degree ultra-wide sensor, 2-megapixel sensor for portrait shots and a low-light camera. The phone has a fingerprint sensor on the back, features a 3D Crystal Feather Glass Design on the back, comes with dedicated dual SIM and microSD card lots and packs a 4000mAh battery.
