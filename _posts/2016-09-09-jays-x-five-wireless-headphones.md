![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/JAYS-x-Five-1-1024x647.jpg) JAYS, a Swedish audio manufacturer has launched their new headphones in India called the JAYS x-Five. The headphones feature a comfortable foldable design with a long 20 hour battery life that makes it excellent for everyday use. The headphones connect over Bluetooth 4.1 with support for SBC and AAC formats. JAYS claim a battery life of 20 hours with a charge time of 2 hours over USB and a wireless range of 10 meters. The headphones feature foldable cups with memory foam for its ear cups and integrated buttons for on-headphone controls. The headphones are available for purchase online on Headphoneszone.in and amazon.in for a price of Rs. 3999 now.
