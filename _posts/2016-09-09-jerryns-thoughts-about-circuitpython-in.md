@jerryn just posted his #CircuitPython2020 thoughts as [a GitHub Gist](https://gist.github.com/jerryneedell/f09d6db0e1689c92bde27618f4548fb7). Click through to hear how things have grown from one of the first folks to join our community. Here is a preview:

> The CircuitPython “family” continues to be a great collection of supportive and collaborative individuals. It is a pleasure to watch the Discord discussions and see the amount of effort people are willing to expend to help newcomers and experienced users with their issues. The commitment to maintaining a friendly environment has paid off tremendously. I am proud to be associated with it.
