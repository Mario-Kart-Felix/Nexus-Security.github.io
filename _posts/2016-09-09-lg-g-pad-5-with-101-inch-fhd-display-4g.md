![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/LG-G-Pad-5.jpg) LG has just announced its new G Pad 5 10.1-inch tablet in South Korea. It is the successor to LG G Pad 4 and the G Pad 5 10.1-inch comes in WiFi-only and an optional 4G LTE variant. The G Pad 5 flaunts a 10.1-inch FULL HD display with a resolution of 1920 x 1200 pixels, metal frame with noticeable bezels, 178-degree wide viewing angle, and is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 821 processor paired with 4GB RAM with 32GB and the storage can be expanded up to 512GB through microSD. It runs on Android 9 (Pie) and sports a side-mounted fingerprint scanner. Regarding the optics, the G Pad 5 10.1-inch sports an 8MP autofocus rear camera and a 5MP front-facing camera. It packs an 8200mAh battery with support for Quick Charge 3.0 and the connectivity options and ports include Dual Band WiFi, Bluetooth 4.2, GPS, USB Type-C port, and 3.5mm headphone jack. It also comes with a comfortable mode to reduce blue light and ease eye strain.
