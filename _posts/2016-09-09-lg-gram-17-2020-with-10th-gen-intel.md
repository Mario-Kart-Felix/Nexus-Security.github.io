![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/LG-Gram-2020-1024x600.jpg) LG electronics first introduced the 17 inch variant of the LG Gram last year with the success of the product leading to almost 25% of LG Gram sales in 2019. Now, for 2020, LG is updating their Gram 17 with the latest 10th Intel processors and a bigger battery, among other changes. The laptop is said to come with a 17 inch screen with a resolution of 2560 x 1600, with a vertical length that's 3mm less than last year. The laptop will have increased battery size at 80WH compared to the 72Watt hour and will weigh total of 1.35 Kilos. The laptop will have extra slots to upgrade the RAM and storage and the keyboard numpad now comes with 4 columns compared to the 3 columns on the 2019 model. LG has also built the laptop to be durable with MIL-STD rating for impact, dust, high temperature, low temperature, vibration, salt and low pressure resistance. The laptop is currently only being released in Korea for a price of 2.12 million won (US$ 1,780 / Rs. 1,27,000 approx.)
