Square Enix joined Microsoft for [its X019 livestream](https://www.gamespot.com/g00/articles/x019-inside-xbox-news-halo-reach-coming-to-mcc-new/1100-6471272/?i10c.ua=4&i10c.encReferrer=aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuZ29vZ2xlLmNvbS8=&i10c.dv=3/) to announce that a plethora of [Final Fantasy](https://www.gamespot.com/final-fantasy/) titles are making their way to Xbox One via Xbox Game Pass next year. Additionally, classic and [Kingdom Hearts](https://www.gamespot.com/kingdom-hearts/) titles will be available on the Xbox platform.

A few classic Final Fantasy titles will arrive on Xbox Game Pass for Console and PC starting sometime next year. These include [Final Fantasy VII](https://www.gamespot.com/final-fantasy-vii/), [Final Fantasy VIII](https://www.gamespot.com/final-fantasy-viii/), [Final Fantasy IX](https://www.gamespot.com/final-fantasy-ix/), [Final Fantasy X](https://www.gamespot.com/final-fantasy-x/), [Final Fantasy X-2](https://www.gamespot.com/final-fantasy-x-2/), and [Final Fantasy XII](https://www.gamespot.com/final-fantasy-xii/). Square Enix also confirmed that some newer entries will be available next year, including [Final Fantasy XIII](https://www.gamespot.com/final-fantasy-xiii/), [Final Fantasy XIII-2](https://www.gamespot.com/final-fantasy-xiii-2/), [Lightning Returns: Final Fantasy XIII](https://www.gamespot.com/lightning-returns-final-fantasy-xiii/), and [Final Fantasy XV](https://www.gamespot.com/final-fantasy-xv/).

Additionally, Square Enix said the Kingdom Hearts HD remastered collections are headed to Xbox One sometime in 2020. These include [Kingdom Hearts 1.5 + 2.5 HD Remix](https://www.gamespot.com/kingdom-hearts-collectors-pack-hd-15-plus-25-remix/) and [Kingdom Hearts HD 2.8 Final Chapter Prologue](https://www.gamespot.com/kingdom-hearts-hd-28-final-chapter-prologue/). Before wrapping up its announcements, Square Enix stated that demo for [Kingdom Hearts III](https://www.gamespot.com/kingdom-hearts-iii/) is available now on Xbox One.

Microsoft's X019 livestream recently concluded. The company divulged a smorgasbord of news and announcements, including an Xbox One release date for [Halo: Reach](https://www.gamespot.com/halo-reach/), the confirmation of [Age of Empires IV](https://www.gamespot.com/age-of-empires-iv/), [a bevy of Xbox Game Pass titles](https://www.gamespot.com/articles/tons-of-new-xbox-game-pass-games-confirmed-some-ou/1100-6471451/) available right now, and much more.
