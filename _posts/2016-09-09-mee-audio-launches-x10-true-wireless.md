![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/MEE-audio-X10-Truly-Wireless-Earbuds.jpg) MEE audio has entered the Indian truly wireless market with the launch of its X10 Truly Wireless earbuds. They feature IPx5 ratings, Bluetooth 5.0 and a total of 23 hours of battery life with the included charging case. The X10 Truly Wireless earbuds have a compact ergonomic design that are in-ear twist-to-fit style of earbuds. It has good noise isolation with the choice to 3 sizes of soft silicone eartips to give the best fit. The IPX5 rating makes it perfect for use in the gym or other workouts. Powering the device are 5mm HD microdrivers that MEE audio claims will give deep bass, warm mid, and crystal clear highs. Connection is taken care of by Bluetooth 5.0 which promises a good stable connection with low latency. Pairing is also fast with smart wireless technology that will auto pair the earbuds as soon as it is taken out of the case. There are integrated controls  media, volume, 1-button access to voice assistants and call with the in- built microphone. For battery life, MEE audio claims a 4.5 hours to continuous listening time with an additional 18 hours stored on the charging case. The charging case can be charged via the included USB-C cable.
