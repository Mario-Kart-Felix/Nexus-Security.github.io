![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/MIUI-11-1-1024x535.jpg) Xiaomi announced the latest iteration of its own proprietary Android skin MIUI, dubbed MIUI 11 along with Mi 9 Pro 5G, Mi MIX Alpha 5G, and Mi AirDots Pro 2 at an event in China earlier this week. MIUI 11 aims to bring brand new optimized modern UI design focused on content viewing. These new visual design and sound effects have won Xiaomi has been awarded two new Red Dot design awards for the new visual design and sound effects on MIUI 11, the company says that it is the first company to receive a first-ever design award in the smartphone software industry. \[gallery size="large" ids="293063,293072,293057"\] As per the schedule shared during the launch, Xiaomi has planned to roll out MIUI 11 (China ROM only) for all the supported devices by November in three batches, while MIUI 11 Public Beta (China ROM) was scheduled to be rolled out for 17 different devices starting September 27. On the launch day itself, MIUI 11 Nightly Beta (China ROM) was leaked for various devices including Redmi K20 Pro and Redmi Note 7 Pro.
