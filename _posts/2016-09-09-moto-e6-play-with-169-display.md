![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Moto-E6-Play-render.jpg) Recently, renders of Motorola Moto G8 Plus (XT2019) smartphone surfaced with FHD+ water-drop notch display, triple rear cameras, and more. Now, a new render of alleged Moto E6 Play smartphone has surfaced revealing key details of the smartphone including the display, camera, colour options, and more. According to render from Mobielkopen, the alleged Moto E6 Play is said to sport a display with 16:9 aspect ratio, but the resolution is not known yet. We can expect it to be an HD display with a resolution of 1280 x 720 pixels. The images also reveal a thick forehead and chin at the bottom. Regarding the optics, this model is said to sport a single rear camera with LED flash next to it and is said to sport a rear-mounted physical fingerprint scanner. Other specifications including the processor, battery, camera resolution, etc are not known yet, but the images just reveal a 3.5mm headphone jack, microphone, micro USB port at the bottom, and power on/off and volume buttons to the sides.
