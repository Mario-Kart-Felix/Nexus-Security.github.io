![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Moto-G8-Play.jpg) After the Moto G8 Plus, Moto G8 Play has surfaced in live images in Brazil. This reveals a 6.2-inch HD+ waterdrop notch screen, triple rear cameras that includes an 117-degree ultra-wide sensor, rear-mounted fingerprint scanner and more. This looks almost similar to the One Macro, but with an ultra-wide sensor instead of macro sensor and no Laser auto focus. Moto G8 Play rumored specifications 6.2-inch (1520 x 720 pixels) Max Vision display Octa Core MediaTek Helio P660 / P70 12nm processor with ARM Mali-G72 MP3 GPU 3GB / 4GB RAM, 32GB / 64GB storage, expandable memory up to 512GB with microSD Hybrid Dual SIM (nano + nano / microSD) Android 9.0 (Pie) 13MP rear camera with LED flash, 117-degree ultra-wide sensor, depth sensor 8MP front-facing camera Fingerprint sensor 3.5mm audio jack, FM Radio Splash resistant (IPX2) Dual 4G VoLTE, WiFi 802.11 b/g/n, Bluetooth 4.2, GPS + GLONASS 4000mAh battery The Moto G8 Play is rumored to be priced at 1,099 Brazilian Real (US$ 264 / Rs. 18,820 approx.), but it should be cheaper in other countries. It is expected to be announced in Brazil on October 24th along with the other G8 series phones. Source
