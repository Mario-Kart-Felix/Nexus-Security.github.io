![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/motorola-one-macro-review_1_1-1024x726.jpg)Starting with One Power, Motorola has been consistently releasing devices with a focus on features that are highlighted with branding. The One Power was all about battery life, the One Vision was all about the low light camera with a big aperture and OIS, the One Action was all about that wide angle action camera and now we have the One Macro. Yes, you guessed it right, it's all about that macro camera, at a relatively micro budget. At Rs. 9,999, the One Macro comes with a 6.2" HD+ screen, is powered by a Helio P70, has 4,000 mAH of battery and comes only in one variant - 4GB RAM, 64GB ROM. Question is - how big of a difference does the Macro Vision camera make, in terms of experience? and overall, is this Motorola One series device worth the asking price? Lets find out in the review. Anyone who has seen or used the earlier Motorola One devices like the One Vision or the One Action will immediately notice the screen is not 21:9.
