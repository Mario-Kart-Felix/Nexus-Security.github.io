![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/04/Motorola-Razr-2019-leak.jpg) Earlier this year, Motorola's foldable smartphone, Motorola Razr (2019) surfaced in press images on Weibo in China and the prior to the that, the patent of the display surfaced too. Now new reports suggest that Motorola could unveil its foldable smartphone by end of this year after delays. Motorola apparently aimed at launching the Razr foldable smartphone during Q3 2019 but missed it. The company is now aiming to unveil the foldable smartphone by the end of this year but the sales might not start this year as Motorola wants the press and the public to see their foldable smartphone first, according to the report. The Razr foldable smartphone will follow Samsung Galaxy Fold and HUAWEI's Mate X 5G foldable smartphone, most likely as the latter hasn't gone on sale yet. According to the press images surfaced earlier this year, Motorola Razr shows resemblance to the iconic Razr handset, but this has a display on the top and the bottom half that expands to reveal a large screen along with a visible hinge and Motorola logo on the back.
