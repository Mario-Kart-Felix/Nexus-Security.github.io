It launched the realme X2 last month starting at Rs. 16,999 with improved specifications, however it did not cut the price of the XT at that time. Now the company has cut the price of the phone by Rs. 1000 on  Flipkart and realme.com. The Rs. 4GB RAM with 64GB storage version is available for Rs. 14999, 6GB RAM with 64GB storage costs Rs. 15,999 and the 8GB RAM with 128GB storage version is available for Rs. 17,999. To remind you, the phone packs a  6.4-inch Full HD+ AMOLED display with 91.9% screen-to-body ratio and Corning Gorilla Glass 5 protection. It is powered by Snapdragon 712 with up to 8GB of RAM, runs Android 9.0 (Pie) with ColorOS 6.0, packs a 64-megapixel primary rear camera with 1/1.72″ Samsung GW1 sensor and f/1.8 aperture, 8MP 118° ultra-wide angle lens with 1.12μm pixel size, f/2.25 aperture, 2MP depth sensor and 2MP camera for 4cm macro with 1.75μm pixel size, f/2.4 aperture.
