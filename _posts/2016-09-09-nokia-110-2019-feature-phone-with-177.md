![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Nokia-110-2019.jpg) HMD Global introduced Nokia 110 (2019) feature phone at IFA event last month and it was announced alongside the Nokia 7.2, Nokia 6.2, Nokia 2720 Flip 4G phone, and Nokia 2720 Flip 4G phone. Today, HMD Global has launched the Nokia 110 (2019) feature phone in India and is positioned as an upgraded version of Nokia 105 (2019) that was launched earlier this year. Nokia 110 (2019) sports a 1.77-inch QQVGA colour display with a resolution of 160 x 120 pixels and is powered by an SPRD 6531E processor paired with 4MB RAM with 4MB internal storage that can be expanded up to 32GB through micro SD card. It runs on Nokia Series 30+ software, has a built-in FM radio giving you access to a range of stations, has a built-in MP3 player, and comes with the Snake game pre-installed along with 4 try and buy games like Ninja Up, Air Strike, Football Cup, Doodle Jump.
