![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Nokia-8.1-Android-10-update-1-1024x706.jpg) HMD Global has started rolling out Android 10 update for its Nokia 8.1, which makes it the first Nokia phone to get the update. It got Android Q beta update long back. Android 10 brings number of additional privacy and security features for users, as well as Dark Theme, Smart Reply, Focus mode, dedicated ‘Privacy section’, and lots more. The Nokia 8.1 Android 10 update V4.15B is 1.44GB in size and still has Android security patch for September. Check out the complete changelog below. Android 10 upgrade for Nokia 8.1 starts today! First on SD710 to bring new features such as Dark theme, Gesture Navigation and more. Staying the course to be the first across the portfolio!
