![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/Nokia-9-PureView_fonearena-3-1024x668.jpg) HMD Global has started rolling out Android 10 update for its flagship Nokia 9 PureView smartphone, which makes it the second Nokia phone to get the update after the Nokia 8.1 in October. Android 10 brings number of additional privacy and security features for users, as well as Dark Theme, Smart Reply, Focus mode, dedicated ‘Privacy section’, and lots more. The Nokia 9 PureView Android 10 update V5.13D is about 1.4GB in size and has Android security patch for November. The company already confirmed  that Nokia 7.1  will get the update in Q4 2019, and the update will continue for older phones in Q1 2020 and Q2 2020. Check out the complete changelog below.
