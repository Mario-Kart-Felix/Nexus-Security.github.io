![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/Nokia-Black-Friday-offers-India-1024x476.png) Black Friday Sale is around the corner and many brands are offering discounts on their product lineup. Similarly now, HMD Global has announced its Black Friday offers in India and users can get up to Rs. 5000 gift card on purchasing select Nokia smartphones. In this Black Friday offer, Rs. 5000 worth gift card is applicable on select smartphones like Nokia 7.2 (4+64GB and 6+64GB), Nokia 6.1 Plus, Nokia 5.1 Plus and Rs. 2000 worth gift card is applicable on purchasing either Nokia 8.1, Nokia 4.2, Nokia 3.1 Plus, Nokia 3.2 (2+16GB and 3+32GB), Nokia 2.2 (2+16GB and 3+32GB). Rs. 4000 worth gift card is applicable on purchasing a Nokia 6.2 smartphone. This offer is valid till December 1st, 2019 and users have to use the code 'GIFTCARD' at the checkout page to avail this offer. The gift card is valid for 30 days from the date of issuance and it can be redeemed on the next purchase from Nokia.com/phones.
