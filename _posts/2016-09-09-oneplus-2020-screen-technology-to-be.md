![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/OnePlus-2020-Screen-Technology-teaser.jpg) OnePlus has sent out invites in China for introducing new 2020 screen technology on January 13. The company has not revealed any details about the event. Based on rumors we can expect 120Hz refresh rate screen, an upgrade from 90Hz screen its current flagships. OnePlus introduced its OnePlus Concept One with hidden camera technology that uses electrochromic glass on the back and a leather finish at the CES earlier this week. Based on recent rumors, the OnePlus 8 and OnePlus 8 Pro smartphones will feature a punch-hole screen with 120Hz refresh rate, so the company is said to preview the screen technology at the China event next week. The OnePlus 2020 Screen Technology takes place at Shenzhen B.PARK bloom garden and begins at 2PM China Time (11:30AM IST). We should know all the details about this technology next Monday. Source
