![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/OnePlus-6T-Thunder-Purple_fonearena-3.jpg) After Oxygen OS Open Beta updates based Android 10 last month, OnePlus just announced that it has started rolling out OxygenOS 10.0 stable update for the OnePlus 6 and OnePlus 6T smartphones. This comes after the OnePlus 7 and OnePlus 7 Pro got the update recently. OnePlus already confirmed that OnePlus 5 and OnePlus 5T users will have to wait till Q2 2020 for the Android 10 update. The update is around 1.8GB in size, As usual, OnePlus said that the OTA will have a staged roll out over the next few days, and using VPN to download the build might not work as the roll out is not based on regions and is randomly pushed out to a limited number of devices. However, you can download the OnePlus 6T update here, if you can't wait.
