![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/OnePlus-7T-Pro-7T-OxygenOS-10.0.4-Cloud-service-1024x745.jpg) OnePlus has started rolling out OxygenOS 10.0.4 update for the OnePlus 7T and 7T Pro smartphones. The update brings 50GB free storage in gallery for India. You can claim the free storage once per account, and is valid for 1 year for the day you claim it, said the company. This was announced at the launch event of the phone. The update also brings several optimizations, including improvements to overall communication performance and improve front camera low-light performance. The OnePlus 7T and 7T Pro OxygenOS 10.0.4 are 99MB and 106MB in size, respectively and are already rolling out to the users. Check out the complete changelog below. System Optimized translation accuracy Optimisedoverall communication (network, phone calls, mobile data) performance General bug fixes and improvements Added Cloud Service for Gallery (India only) Camera Optimised quality of the front camera for better pictures at night Source
