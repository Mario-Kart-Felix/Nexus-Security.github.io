![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/OnePlus-McLaren.jpg) OnePlus partnered with McLaren for the OnePlus 6T McLaren edition last year. Now the company has started teasing the launch of new McLaren edition phone, which should be the OnePlus 7T Pro McLaren Edition. The company said that the OnePlus 6T McLaren edition sold-out across the world in a matter of weeks. Based on earlier rumors, the 7T Pro McLaren Edition is expected to pack 12GB of RAM and could feature a similar carbon fiber finish under the glass and light streaks across the McLaren logo, illuminating and disappearing in an instant. Other specifications should be similar to the OnePlus 7T Pro, but it is not clear if we can expect a faster charging technology compared to the Warp Charge 30T used in OnePlus 7T. We should know the price and other details at the October 10th event in London. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2fc4IBbCM50 Pete Lau, Founder and CEO of OnePlus, said: We’ve long been inspired by the McLaren brand spirit as an established company that continues to innovate. The public reaction to the OnePlus 6T McLaren edition was incredible, users enjoyed seeing us working together to push boundaries, and we are thrilled to continue working together.
