![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/OnePlus-7T_fonearena-02-1024x661.jpg) OnePlus has come a long way from launching a single device to four devices in multiple segments in a single year. OnePlus 7T is the third device from OnePlus this year with some significant upgrade (from OnePlus 7) in terms of design, display, and camera. The OnePlus 7T acts as a bridging device between OnePlus 7 and OnePlus 7 Pro both in terms of price and feature, giving users a perfect option to choose from. Priced at Rs. 37,999, OnePlus is back to its territory where it ruled the charts with OnePlus 6, 6T and 7. With several options available from OnePlus itself apart from its competition, is it wise to spend on the 7T?
