![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/OnePlus-8-Lite-1024x875.jpg) OnePlus recently launched the OnePlus 7T and OnePlus 7T Pro as an upgrade to OnePlus 7 and 7 Pro. The leaks and renders of the upcoming OnePlus 8 and OnePlus 8 Pro have surfaced already and according to the latest report, OnePlus could be working on an affordable version of OnePlus 8 and it could be called OnePlus 8 Lite. According to the renders, OnePlus 8 Lite is said to be a mid-range smartphone with a flat display with a noticeable chin at the bottom and curved rear glass. Unlike the 7 Pro/7T Pro models, this device will not sport a curved display and the display size, and the resolution is not known yet. The renders also reveal the alert slider and power button to the right, volume rockers to the left, USB Type-C port, and the speaker grille. Regarding the optics, the renders reveal a dual-camera setup housed in a rectangular camera module and according to the images, OnePlus 8 Lite could also feature laser auto focus and a time-of-flight (ToF) sensor.
