![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/OnePlus-8-Pro-leaked-render.jpg) It's been just over a few weeks since the launch of OnePlus 7T and OnePlus 7T Pro and now the renders of the upcoming OnePlus 8 Pro has started to surface online. OnePlus 8 Pro renders come after the renders of OnePlus 8 that surfaced recently. According to the render by @OnLeaks, OnePlus 8 Pro is said to sport a 6.65-inch Quad HD+ 90Hz display with curved edges and a similar design language as OnePlus 8. This time around OnePlus is said to go with a punch-hole display instead of the pop-up camera module and also, the display is said to be slightly smaller than its predecessor OnePlus 7T Pro. While the processor that is going to power OnePlus 8 and OnePlus 8 Pro is not known yet, we can expect it to be powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 865 processor paired with 8GB RAM with 256GB storage. The renders also reveal a rear quad-camera setup on OnePlus 8 Pro and going by the images, it is said to be three rear cameras in vertical alignment along with a 3D ToF sensor, laser autofocus sensor placed next to it.
