![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/OnePlus-Concept-One-CES-2020-1024x570.jpg) Last week OnePlus confirmed that it will hold a special event at the CES 2020 in Las Vegas next month. Today OnePlus CEO Pete Lau on weibo confirmed that the company will introduce its first concept smartphone, which it calls OnePlus Concept One will be introduced at the event between January 7th and 10th, 2020. He said that it will be a futuristic device, but did not say how it will look. The company is celebrating its sixth anniversary since the launch of the first OnePlus One smartphone. So @oneplus is hosting a Special Event during #CES2020 in Las Vegas Watch this space! pic.twitter.com/cX4tpjKdKi — FoneArena Mobile (@FoneArena) December 13, 2019 OnePlus makes a lot of prototypes before it finalizes the design, but this will be the company's first concept phone that will be introduced officially to the public. The phone will likely support 5G and it is not clear if this will be foldable smartphone. We should know more details about the OnePlus concept phone in the coming weeks. Source
