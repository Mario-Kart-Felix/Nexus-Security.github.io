![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/OnePlus-120Hz-refresh-rate-display-1.jpg) OnePlus at its 2020 Screen Technology event introduced Quad HD+ OLED screen with a refresh rate of 120Hz, 240Hz touch sampling rate, 10-bit HDR, as it had promised. At the event it said that it accounted for 77% of the domestic sales of 90Hz refresh rate phones online. OnePlus said that each screen is calibrated at the factory for accurate colors with RGB color temperature sensor with <0.8 Just Noticeable Color Difference (JNCD) rating.  It offers 4096-level (Up from 1024-level) automatic brightness adjustment, highest in Android. It can also be adjusted according to ambient light, multi-scene auto dimming, screen color temperature dimming and so on. It also features built-in MEMC motion compensation chip that is commonly seen in TVs to avoid motion blur. OnePlus has also shared a comparison chart that compares that screen response between different phones, and says that the company's latest screen offers better response time. The OnePlus 120Hz Quad HD+ AMOLED screen is expected to be used in the OnePlus 8 Pro that is expected to be launched sometime in Q2 2020.
