![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/OPPO-A11.jpg) OPPO recently launched the A9 2020 and A5 2020 smartphones in India and yesterday, the A9 2020 4GB RAM variant got a price cut in India. After the launch of both smartphones, OPPO has announced its new smartphone in China - OPPO A11 which is almost identical to OPPO A5 2020. In terms of design and specifications, OPPO A11 is similar to OPPO A5 2020, except for variation in RAM and storage size. Other specifications including the display, processor, battery, etc remain the same. OPPO A11 flaunts a 6.5-inch Nano Waterdrop Screen with a resolution of 1600 x 720 pixels, is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 665 processor paired with 4GB RAM with 128GB internal storage and the storage can be expanded up to 256GB with microSD. Regarding the optics, OPPO A11 sports quad-rear cameras with a combination of 12MP (primary with f/1.8 aperture) + 8MP (ultra-wide-angle lens with f/2.25 aperture) + 2MP depth sensor with f/2.4 aperture + 2MP lens for 4cm macro with f/2.4 aperture as well.
