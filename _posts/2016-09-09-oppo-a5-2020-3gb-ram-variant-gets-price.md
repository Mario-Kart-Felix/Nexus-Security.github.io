![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/OPPO-A5-2020.jpg) OPPO launched the A9 (2020) and A5 (2020) smartphones last month in India.  Following the price cut OPPO A9 2020 4GB RAM variant in India recently, OPPO A5 2020 3GB RAM variant has now got a price cut in India. OPPO A5 (2020) 3GB RAM with 64GB storage model has received a Rs. 500 permanent price cut and is now available for Rs. 11,990, against its launch price of Rs. 12,490. However, there is no price cut in the 4GB RAM variant and it is selling for Rs. 13,990. The revised prices are reflecting on Amazon.in and opposhop.in. Smartphone Old Price New Price Price cut OPPO A5 (2020) Rs. 12,490(3+64GB) Rs. 11,990(3+64GB) Rs. 500 To recall, OPPO A5 (2020) flaunts a 6.5-inch Nano Waterdrop Screen, is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 665 processor paired with 3GB/4GB RAM with 64GB internal storage. Regarding the optics, it has quad-rear cameras with a combination of 12MP (primary) + 8MP (ultra-wide-angle) + 2MP (depth sensor) + 2MP (macro). There is an 8MP front camera and the rear cameras support EIS and Ultra Night Mode 2.0. It also has support for  Dolby Atmos audio, Dual Stereo Speakers and packs a 5000mAh battery with reverse charging capability.
