![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/OPPO-A9-2020-2.jpg) OPPO recently launched the A9 2020 smartphone in India alongside the A5 2020 with quad rear cameras, 5000mAh battery, and more. Just after a month of launch in India, now, OPPO A9 2020 has received a price cut and this time around, it is the 4GB RAM variant and the price of 8GB RAM remains unchanged. The A9 2020 has received a Rs. 1000 price cut and after the price cut, the 4GB RAM with 128GB storage version is available for Rs. 15,990, against its original price of Rs. 16,990. However, the 8GB RAM with 128GB storage version costs Rs. 19,990. The revised prices are reflecting on Amazon.in and offline stores as well. Smartphone Old Price New Price Price cut OPPO A9 2020 Rs. 16,990(4+64GB) Rs. 15,990(4+64GB) Rs. 1000 To recall, OPPO A9 2020 flaunts a 6.5-inch Nano Waterdrop Screen, is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 665 processor, and sports Quad rear cameras with a 48-megapixel main camera, 8MP 119° ultra-wide-angle lens, 2MP depth sensor, and 2MP for 4cm macro. There is a 16-megapixel front camera with support for EIS and Ultra Night Mode 2.0. It also has Dolby Atmos, Dual Stereo Speakers and packs a 5000mAh battery with reverse charging capability.
