Incredible art news from [Parise Musées](http://www.parismusees.paris.fr/sites/default/files/medias/fichiers/2020-01/CP%20Paris%20Mus%C3%A9es%20Open%20Content%20EN.pdf):

> From 8 January 2020, Paris Musées is offering as Open Content (i.e. making available  
> without charge and without restrictions) 100,000 digital reproductions in High  
> Definition of works in the City’s museums.

[Read more](http://www.parismusees.paris.fr/sites/default/files/medias/fichiers/2020-01/CP%20Paris%20Mus%C3%A9es%20Open%20Content%20EN.pdf) and [see more](http://parismuseescollections.paris.fr/en)
