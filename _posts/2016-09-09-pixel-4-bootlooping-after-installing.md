TWRP won't be ready for the Pixel 4 and 4 XL until months after release. Luckily, there's still a way around this. This method of recovery uses a core-only version of Magisk to disable all modules so you can boot back into Android. XDA member Tulsadiver has provided a set of Pixel 4 and 4 XL image files to help you recover from that bootloop.
