date: 2019-10-13T03:53:00+01:00
draft: false
---

Damayantiben Modi, PM Narendra Modi’s niece is the latest victim in the string of snatching incidents in the capital. Damayantiben was getting off an auto-rickshaw at Raj Niwas Marg on Saturday morning when the criminals struck. They fled with her bag which contained Rs 56,000 in cash, two mobile phones, her family’s flight tickets for Ahmedabad and her ID cards.
