![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Portronics_Auto-10.jpg) Portronics, the brand known for its wide range of audio products, car accessories etc has today launched its new 'Auto 10' car charger cum smart audio connector in India. It has Bluetooth 5.0 adapter, in-built microphone, mini FM transmitting station and more. The 'Auto 10' dual-purpose car accessory is suited for daily car commuters and long-distance travellers and also acts as a smart audio connector especially the ones who do not have a car stereo system. This dual-purpose car accessory sports dual USB ports that allow users to charge their USB devices with up to 12/24V - 3.4A and users can also plug-in a USB flash drive to play songs, videos, etc. There is a MicroSD slot that supports card up to 32GB and it also an in-built microphone. Apart from this, there are two buttons on this car accessory - Bass Boost button and Multifunction button. Users can use the Bass Boost button for increasing the Bass and use the Multifunction function to play/pause/ answer/reject calls. It connects through the car's cigarette lighter and the Bluetooth 5.0 adaptor on this device syncs with the user's smartphone Bluetooth and the mini FM transmitting station connects to the car's FM receiver.
