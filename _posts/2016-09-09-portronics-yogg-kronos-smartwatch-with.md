![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/Portronics-Yogg-Kronos_1-1024x819.jpg) Portronics, the portable gadget manufacturer has launched yet another smartwatch called the Yogg Kronos. This smartwatch cum fitness tracker is equipped with touch-enabled 1.3-inch colour screen that helps you track your heart rate, steps, sleep time and more. Synchronising data using the VeryFit Pro App, the Yogg Kronos can track a multitude of fitness related data such as steps, calories, distance, sleep times. The smartwatch can also set reminders, sync social apps, emails, and missed calls. The lightweight device weighs only 40.8 grams and is rated for IP68 water and dust resistance making it ideal for all seasons, rain or wind. It features a magnetic charger that will charge its 210mAH battery. Portronics promises a battery life of about 8-10 days with the Yogg Kronos. The Portronics Yogg Kronos will be available for a price of Rs. 3999 on online and offline retail stores throughout India, but you can get it from Amazon for as low as Rs. 3499 and has been listed on Flipkart for Rs. 2999.
