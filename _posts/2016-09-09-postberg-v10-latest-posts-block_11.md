Pro Latest Posts Block allows you to display your latest blog posts as a beautiful grid /list/slider view. The Pro Latest Posts Block gives you quick access to a beautiful post grid, post list and slider view that can be added to any post or page on your website. The post grid can be configured to toggle on and off the post’s date, read more button, and also to set up a custom image height for the post’s featured image.
