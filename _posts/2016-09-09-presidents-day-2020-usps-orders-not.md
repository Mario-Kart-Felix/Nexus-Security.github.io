Adafruit will not be shipping USPS orders Monday February 17, 2020 for the federal holiday, Presidents Day.

Expedited USPS orders placed after 11am ET Friday February 14 will go out Tuesday February 18.

Visit [Adafruit.com/Holiday](https://www.adafruit.com/holiday) for Adafruit holiday shipping info and [Adafruit.com/ShippingInfo](https://www.adafruit.com/shippinginfo) for a comprehensive overview of shipping options and policies.
