[Fantasy Map Generator](https://github.com/Azgaar/Fantasy-Map-Generator) is a free [open source](https://github.com/Azgaar/Fantasy-Map-Generator/blob/master/LICENSE) tool which procedurally generates fantasy maps. You may use auto-generated maps as they are, edit them or even create a new map from scratch. Check out the [quick start tutorial](https://github.com/Azgaar/Fantasy-Map-Generator/wiki/Quick-Start-Tutorial) and [Q&A](https://github.com/Azgaar/Fantasy-Map-Generator/wiki/Q&A) for guidance.

Join our [Discord server](https://discordapp.com/invite/X7E84HU) and [Reddit community](https://www.reddit.com/r/FantasyMapGenerator/) to ask questions, get help and share created maps. You may support the project on [Patreon](https://www.patreon.com/azgaar).

The project is under active development. For older versions see the [changelog](https://github.com/Azgaar/Fantasy-Map-Generator/wiki/Changelog). To track the development progress see the [devboard](https://trello.com/b/7x832DG4/fantasy-map-generator). Please report bugs [here](https://github.com/Azgaar/Fantasy-Map-Generator/issues). You can also contact me directly via [email](mailto:azgaar.fmg@yandex.by).
