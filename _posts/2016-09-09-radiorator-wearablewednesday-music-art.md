Intriguing art project from [Agustina](https://vimeo.com/agustinable) on [Vimeo](https://vimeo.com).

> Radiorator helmet is a wearable that allows to hear the radio frequencies of the broad spectrum. With an antenna the user receive the frequencies according with their own movements allowing the possibility to interact with the environment. The radio frequencies are digitally decode in sounds and the performer make a music set.
