![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/realme-Power-Bank-Classic-Blue-1024x689.jpg) Earlier this month realme introduced the 10,000mAh realme Power Bank in Classic Blue after launching it in Grey, Red and Yellow color variants last year. Today it has confirmed that the new color variant will go on sale from January 26th on Flipkart and realme.com at Rs. 1299. The Blue version has yellow finish on the top and the bottom, which makes it unique from other version that have black finish. It retains all the features such as 18W two-way fast charging, dual output and 12 layers of protection. realme 10000mAh power bank specifications 10000mAh (typical) lithium-polymer battery Dual output USB Type-A and USB Type-C 18W (5V-3A/9V-2A/12V-1.5A) two-way fast charging via USB Type-C USB Type-A Output (5V-3A/9V-2A/12V-1.5A) 12 layers of protection including current, over-power, short circuit, over charger or discharge and more 12.5mm thick, Weight: 230 grams Power bank is charged in 3.28 hours with 18W charger and in 5.36 hours with 10W charger
