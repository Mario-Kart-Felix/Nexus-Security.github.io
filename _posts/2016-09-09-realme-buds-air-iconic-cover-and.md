![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Realme-Buds-Air-Iconic-Cover-Classic-Blue-1024x559.jpg) realme launched the realme Buds Air in India few weeks back in Black, White and Yellow colors. Since the case is glossy it attracted fingerprints and is prone to scratches if you store it along with sharp objects. Today at the realme 5i launch event the company has launched a new Iconic Cover for the Buds Air. This comes in Classic Blue color, which the company calls Limited Edition color. The Iconic Cover for the realme Buds Air features impact resistant, form fitting design offering complete protection against falls and slip-offs. This doesn't affect the wireless charging. The 1000mAh realme Power Bank in Classic Blue joins the Grey, Red and Yellow color variants, and retains all the features such as 18W two-way fast charging, dual output and 12 layers of protection. Both these are made of eco-friendly material that is smooth and durable and will go on sale during the Republic Day Sale later this month. The 1000mAh realme Power Bank should be priced same as the other colors, which is Rs. 1,299, and the Realme Buds Air Iconic Cover should be revealed closer to the sale date.
