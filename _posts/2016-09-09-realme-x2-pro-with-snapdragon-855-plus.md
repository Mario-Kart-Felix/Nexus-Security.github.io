![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/realme-X2-Pro-1024x628.jpg) After rumors, realme has confirmed that it will introduce realme X2 Pro with Snapdragon 855 Plus soon. This was confirmed by realme China CMO Xu Qi Chase on weibo. He also said that the phone is codenamed Super Warrior. realme China product director Wang Wei Derek confirmed that the phone will come with 90Hz display refresh rate. The screenshot also shows NFC support for the phone. He recently confirmed that it will be a 4G phone and will not come with 5G support. The screenshot confirms Full HD+ (2400 x 1080 pixels) 20:9 aspect ratio display for the phone. The display size has not been revealed yet, but it should be AMOLED panel with support for an in-display fingerprint scanner. We don't have any other details of the phone, but it could come with improved cameras and the same 30W fast charging as the X2. It has just been a week since the company introduced the realme X2 smartphone with Snapdragon 730G in China, which will launch in India as realme XT 730G in December. Source 1, 2, 3
