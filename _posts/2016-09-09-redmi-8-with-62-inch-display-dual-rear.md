![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/Redmi-8-8A-M1908C3IC.jpg) Redmi 8 with model number M1908C3IC got certified by TENAA in China last month which revealed its specifications. At the end of Redmi 8A online launch, Xiaomi India head and VP Manu Jain asks "But, What about the Redmi 8, weren't we supposed to launch it today?" He gets a response saying that it's for the next launch. May be we can expect the details about the Redmi Note 8 series at the Redmi 8 launch since those are also expected in October. Based on earlier rumors, the phone will feature a 6.26-inch HD+ Dot Notch screen, feature a 12-megapixel rear camera, which could be the same Sony IMX363 sensor, 1.4μm pixel size, f/1.9 aperture sensor Dual PD auto focus featured in the Redmi 8A, and also adds 5-megapixel depth sensor. Processor details are not know yet, but it will pack the same 5000mAh  battery with 18W fast charging. This is also said to feature a rear-mounted fingerprint scanner.
