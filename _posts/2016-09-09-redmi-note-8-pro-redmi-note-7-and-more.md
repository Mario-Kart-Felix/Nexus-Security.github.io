![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Redmi-Note-8-Pro_fonearena-01-1024x633.jpg) Xiaomi announced Android 10 update roadmap for its devices back in June, but it was only limited to MIUI China ROM. The company rolled out Android 10 based MIUI 10 to Redmi K20 Pro users in India and China on 5th September, the day Google rolled out Android 10 to Pixel devices. Xiaomi is yet to roll out Android 10 update for its Android One series of devices, Mi A3, Mi A2, and Mi A2 Lite, but the company has started rolling out Android 10 based MIUI 11 update to Redmi K20 (Mi 9T) users. Xiaomi has already completed MIUI 11 roll-out for all 27 supported devices and now the company has started rolling out Android 10 update to a few devices. The devices receiving Android 10 based MIUI 11 update are, Redmi Note 8 Pro Redmi Note 7 Mi MIX 3 Mi 9 SE Mi 8 SE Note that the Android 10 update is in the testing phase and is being rolled out to MIUI China ROM users only as of now.
