![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Reliance-Jio-All-in-one-plans.jpg) Reliance Jio recently started charging 6 paise per minute for voice calls made to non-Jio mobile networks. Today it has introduced new all-in-one plans that 2 GB/day along with unlimited voice, SMS, Apps as well as free additional 1000 minutes of offnet IUC calling to additional operators worth Rs. 80. Jio's existing 3-month 2GB per day pack will now cost only Rs 444 instead of 448 with additional 1000 min of offnet IUC min. 2-month plan costs Rs. 333 against earlier cost of 396 with the same 1000 min of offnet IUC min. Plans Validity Total data (GB) Voice Benefits 222 28 days 56GB at 2GB/day Jio to Jio Unlimited Jio to Non-Jio FUP of 1,000 minutes 333 56 days 112GB at 2GB/day 444 84 days 168GB at 2GB/day 555 84 days 168GB at 2GB/day Jio to Jio Unlimited Jio to Non-Jio FUP of 3,000 minutes Reliance Jio says that it's plans are still 20-50% cheaper than existing competition plans. In comparison, Airtel offers 2GB/day 82-day plan at Rs. 499, however you get unlimited calls to any network, unlike Jio.
