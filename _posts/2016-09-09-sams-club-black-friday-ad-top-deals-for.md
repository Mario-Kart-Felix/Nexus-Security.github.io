Sam’s Club may be where you stock up on food and beverage essentials, however, the retailer just dropped its [Black Friday 2019 ad](https://www.samsclub.com/content/black-friday-catalog?xid=hdr_banner) and there are many deals on consoles, laptops, and other electronics that you don’t want to miss.

  

Before you get ready for the big shopping day, [our sister site BestBlackFriday.com](https://bestblackfriday.com/ads/sams-club-black-friday) points out that hours for Sam’s Club are a bit different compared to other retailers. Sam’s Club stores will be closed on Thanksgiving Day (Nov. 28) and doors will officially open at 7 a.m. local time on [Black Friday (Nov. 29)](https://www.geek.com/deals-2/the-weirdest-black-friday-2019-deals-1810237/). Plus, if you want to get a head start on your holiday list, all of the deals featured in the Sam’s Club Black Friday ad will be available online on Thanksgiving Day.

  

The ad is a mid-sized Black Friday preview, but some important items that will be on sale include the Nintendo Switch bundle, the Samsung Curved 65-inch 4K TV, and the HP 15.6-inch laptop.

  

Below are the top gaming and tech deals from the Sam’s Club Black Friday ad.
