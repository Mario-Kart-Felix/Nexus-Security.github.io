![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/Samsung-Galaxy-Anniversary-Premium-Package-1.jpg) To celebrate 10 years of the Galaxy series, Samsung has released its Samsung Galaxy Anniversary Premium Package, showcasing the seamless connectivity in its Galaxy ecosystem with Galaxy Note 10 and Galaxy Note 10+. The Galaxy Anniversary Premium package will come with a Galaxy Note10+, Galaxy Watch Active2, and Galaxy Buds. With this anniversary, Samsung is trying to highlight the connectivity between the devices in the Samsung ecosystem. With SmartScreen, users can simply send content straight from their Galaxy phone to their Samsung Smart TV with just a tap. With Wireless PowerShare, you can charge your Galaxy Buds on-the-go. With Link to Windows, you to connect seamlessly to your Windows 10 PC right from the control panel.  And with Samsung Health on both the Galaxy Note10 and Galaxy Watch Active2, you can easily track your progress towards your health and wellness goals. The Samsung Galaxy Anniversary Premium Package will be available in select markets starting from November. In the US it will be available from December 3 on Samsung.com. Source
