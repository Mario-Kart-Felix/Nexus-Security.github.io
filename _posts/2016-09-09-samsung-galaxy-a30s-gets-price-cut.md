![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/Samsung-Galaxy-A30s-1-1024x711.jpg) Samsung launched the Galaxy A30s smartphone back in September this year and it got a price cut last month alongside the Galaxy A50s. After a month of first price cut, now the Galaxy A30s has got another price cut here. After the price cut, Samsung Galaxy A30s is now available for Rs. 14,999, against its previous price of Rs. 15,999. The Galaxy A30s comes in a single variant i.e., 4GB RAM with 64GB of internal storage, but there are reports that Samsung will launch the 128GB storage variant soon. The revised prices are reflecting on Amazon.in and Samsung India eStore. Smartphone Launch Price Old Price New Price Price Cut Samsung Galaxy A30s Rs. 16,999 Rs. 15,999 Rs. 14,999 Rs. 1000 To recall, Samsung Galaxy A30s flaunts a 6.4-inch HD+ Infinity-V Super AMOLED screen, is powered by Exynos 7904 octa-core 14nm SoC with 4GB of RAM and runs Android 9 Pie. It sports a 25-megapixel rear camera with f/1.7 aperture along with an 8-megapixel 123° ultra-wide and 5-megapixel depth sensor and a 16-megapixel front camera with f/2.0 aperture. It packs a 4000mAh battery with 15W fast charging. Via
