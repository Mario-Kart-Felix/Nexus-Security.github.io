![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Samsung-Galaxy-Book-Flex-Alpha-1024x782.jpg) Ahead of CES 2020, Samsung has introduced the Galaxy Book Flex α (alpha), a new Galaxy Book Flex with 13.3-inch QLED display super-bright display with max 600-nit brightness, 100% color volume, ultra-thin bezel, thin and light design weighing 1.19kg and is 13.9mm thick. The Galaxy Book Flex α features a sharp, diamond-cut edges and a crisp, durable aluminum frame. Under the hood, it is powered by 10th generation Intel Core Processor, offers Active Pen support and promises 17.5-hour battery life with support for Fast Charge feature. Samsung Galaxy Book Flex α specifications 13.3" QLED  (1920 x 1080 pixels) FHD Display 10th generation Intel Core Processor with Intel UHD Graphics 8GB / 12GB DDR4 RAM, 256GB / 512GB / Up to 1TB SSD (NVMe), MicroSD 720p HD camera, Dual Array Digital Mic Stereo Speakers (1.5 W x 2) Active Pen (sold separately) Fingerprint sensor Wi-Fi 6 (Gig+), 802.11ax 2x2, USB-C,  2 x USB 3.0, HDMI Dimensions: 304.9 x 202.0 x 13.9 mm; Weight: 1.19 kg 54 Wh (typical) battery with up to  17.5-hour battery life, fast charging The Samsung Galaxy Book Flex α comes in Royal Silver colors, starts at $829.99 (Rs. 59,411 approx.) and will roll out in the first half of 2020.
