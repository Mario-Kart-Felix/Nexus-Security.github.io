![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Samsung-Galaxy-Note-10-Lite-India-launch-date.jpg) After teasing the phone and 'Notifiy me' page on its website, Samsung has confirmed that it will launch the Galaxy Note10 Lite smartphone in India on January 21st. To remind you the phones pack a 6.7-inch Full HD+ Super AMOLED Infinity-O Display, in-display fingerprint scanner, 32-megapixel front camera inside the tiny punch-hole and a 4500mAh battery with support for 25w fast charging. It features the signature Bluetooth S Pen with Air Commands. Powered by Exynos 9810 SoC, it has a 3.5mm audio jack,  12MP main camera with OIS, 12MP ultra-wide and a 12MP Telephoto camera.
