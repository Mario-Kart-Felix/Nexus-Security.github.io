![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Samsung-Galaxy-S10-Lite-and-Note-10-Lite-1.jpg) After rumors Samsung just announced the Galaxy S10 Lite and Note10 Lite smartphones ahead of CES 2020 next week. These pack a 6.7-inch Full HD+ Super AMOLED Plus Infinity-O Display, in-display fingerprint scanner, 32-megapixel front camera inside the tiny punch-hole and a 4500mAh battery with support for 25w fast charging. The Galaxy Note10 Lite features the signature Bluetooth S Pen with Air Commands. The S10 Lite is powered by Snapdragon 855 and the Note10 Lite packs Exynos 9810 SoC used in the S9 and Note9. The S10 Lite packs a 48MP main camera with OIS, 12MP ultra-wide and a 5MP macro camera, while the Note10 Lite comes with 12MP main camera with OIS, 12MP ultra-wide and a 12MP Telephoto camera.
