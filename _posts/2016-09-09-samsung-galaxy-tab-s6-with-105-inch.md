![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Samsung-Galaxy-Tab-S6-1024x738.jpg) Samsung has launched the Galaxy Tab S6, successor of last year’s Galaxy Tab S4 in India. It has a 10.5-inch Super AMOLED WQXGA screen, is powered by a Octa-Core Snapdragon 855 SoC and runs on Android 9.0 (Pie). It has quad-stereo speakers tuned by AKG by HARMAN, similar to the predecessor and has got rid of the Iris scanner in favor of in-display fingerprint scanner. The Galaxy  Tab S6 has an 8-megapixel primary camera along with a 5-megapixel 123-degree  Ultra Wide camera and an 8-megapixel front camera. It has a 5.7mm slim metal body. The tablet comes with an enhanced S Pen that comes with remote control functionality and wireless charging capabilities with built-in BLE remote control features for S Pen Air actions to take selfies and videos, or control the multimedia content. It has a built-in 0.35mAh battery with wireless charging when you stick it to the back of the tablet that promises up to 10 hours of battery life with ten minutes of charging. A new-and-improved version of Samsung DeX  lets you seamlessly transition to a PC-like experience.
