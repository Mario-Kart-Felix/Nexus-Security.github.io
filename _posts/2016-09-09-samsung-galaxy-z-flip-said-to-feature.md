![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/Samsung-Galaxy-Fold-2-leak-1024x520.jpg) Samsung is all set to introduce its second-generation foldable smartphone at the Unpacked 2020 on February 11. Now details about the Galaxy Z Flip (Previously called Galaxy Bloom) have started surfacing. Earlier rumors said that the phone will feature a 6.7-inch foldable Dynamic AMOLED display and a cover display. According to the latest rumors, the Galaxy Z Flip will feature 'Samsung Ultra Thin Glass', and there will be crease when the screen folds. The rumor also says that the phone will be powered by Snapdragon 855 Plus, and not the Snapdragon 865. It is said to come with 1-inch front display lets you check charging speeds and battery info and will also work as a view finder for the camera for better rear camera selfies. The phone is said to feature dual 12MP rear cameras, one wide and an ultra-wide cameras, side-mounted fingerprint scanner, 15W charging as well as support for wireless charging and reverse wireless charging. Earlier rumors revealed Black and Purple colors for the phone. We should know more details about the phone in the coming days. Source
