[Samsung India outsources production of its smartphone models](https://varindia.com/news/samsung-india-outsources-production-of-its-smartphone-models#.XbawpGNcSGk.blogger): According to latest reports, Samsung India has outsourced production of some of its smartphone models to a Sino-India contract manufacturing joint venture, as part of its plans to make the South Asian nation a hub for servicing its local and global clients.
