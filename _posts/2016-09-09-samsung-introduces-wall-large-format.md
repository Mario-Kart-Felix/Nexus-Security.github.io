![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/Samsung-The-Wall.gif) Is your 75 inch TV not big enough? Maybe 4k resolution is not crisp enough for you? Samsung may have the solution with The Wall. Designed with its modular MicroLED display, The Wall comes with the size option of 146 inch (370.8 cm) in 4K definition, 219 inch (556.3 cm) in 6K definition to 292 inch (741.7 cm) in 8K definition. There is no doubt this TV is meant only for those craving absolute luxury for their homes. The TV has a depth less than 30mm, the slim, bezel-less infinity design along with customisable deco frames that allows the display to blend seamlessly into its surroundings, ensuring no disruption to the desired ambience. Thanks to MicroLED technology, the TV can achieve true black and comes with support for and AI up-scaling, Quantum HDR technology, peak brightness of 2,000 nits and 120Hz video rate, ideal for gaming on console or PC. MicroLED also comes with a 100,000-hour lifetime, which allows The Wall  to never be turned off and change into a digital canvas that best matches the owner\`s interior needs and personal mood.
