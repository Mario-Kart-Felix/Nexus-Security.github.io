![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/Samsung-W20-TENAA.jpg) Samsung already confirmed that the new W20 5G foldable smartphone will be introduced on November 19th in China. The teaser showed a similar design as the Galaxy Fold, now the phone with the model number SM-W2020 has been certified by TENAA confirming a similar design and almost similar specifications as the Fold, except for the slightly lass battery.
