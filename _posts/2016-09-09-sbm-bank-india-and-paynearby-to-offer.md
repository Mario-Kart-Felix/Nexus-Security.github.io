[SBM Bank India and PayNearby to offer ‘open banking’ to the masses](https://varindia.com/news/sbm-bank-india-and-paynearby-to-offer-open-banking-to-the-masses#.XhL_p9WSN38.blogger): SBM Bank (India), the first bank to receive banking licence in India through wholly owned subsidiary route has announced a tie up with PayNearby, India’s largest hyperlocal fintech start up. Both firms signed a Memorandum of Understanding to build an “open banking” network towards delivering basic banking solutions as well as advanced financial services, to their customers.
