date: 2019-12-18T01:40:00+01:00
draft: false
---

There are vulnerabilities in the Linux kernel to which the IBM FlashSystem![™](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/11/72x72/2122.png) V840 and FlashSystem V9000 are susceptible (CVE-2019-11477, CVE-2019-11478, and CVE-2019-11479). An exploit of these vulnerabilities could allow a remote attacker to cause a denial of service condition.

**Affected product(s) and affected version(s):**

Affected Product(s)

Version(s)

SVC

7.8.1.10

Flash

1.6.1.0

Flash

1.5.2.5

SVC

8.2.1.5
