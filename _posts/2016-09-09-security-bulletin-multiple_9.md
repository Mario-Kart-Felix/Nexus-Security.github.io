There are multiple vulnerabilities in the IBM® Runtime Environment Java![™](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/11/72x72/2122.png) Version 7 or version 8 used by Financial Transaction Manager for Corporate Payment Services for Multi-Platform (FTM CPS). Financial Transaction Manager for Corporate Payment Services for Multi-Platform has addressed the applicable CVEs.

Affected Products and Versions
------------------------------

FTM CPS: v3.0.2.0 – 3.0.2.1, v3.2.1.0
