An important aspect of a system is how it behaves under different conditions and inputs. To model system behavior, Terrastruct allows you to create Scenario's for your diagram. Each Scenario is meant to capture a different set of conditions, and is composed of multiple Frame's, so that the steps a system takes as it responds to the varying conditions can be explained individually.
