![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Sony-NW-A105-Walkman-1.png) Sony Walkman was a big hit back then and was a goto choice of music player for many users. Over the years, smartphones have taken over the place of MP3 players, but that doesn't stop Sony from bringing back the Walkman in a new avatar. After a long gap, Sony has now launched the NW-A105 Walkman and this time around, it comes with Hi-Res Audio and Android 9 Pie. Sony NW-A105 sports a 3.6-inch HD touch screen display, features 4GB RAM with 16GB of internal storage which can be expanded up to 128GB through microSD. It supports Hi-Res Audio, DSD, FLAC audio formats, and S-Master HX digital amplifier for reduced distortion and noise. It also has convenient side controls for media playback. Regarding the connectivity, the Walkman supports WiFi, Bluetooth, NFC, 3.5mm audio jack and the best part is that it runs on Android 9 Pie. Users can stream tour favourite songs online or download directly on NW-A105 Walkman. Lastly, it promises to deliver 26 hours of battery life on a single charge and can be charged through the USB Type-C port.
