![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Sony-Xperia-5-Plus-leak-1-1024x802.jpg) Sony announced the Xperia 5 smartphone with 21:9 OLED display, triple rear cameras and the company recently rolled out Android 10 for the same. Following that launch, now, the renders successor to the Xperia 5 has surfaced - Sony Xperia 5 Plus along with key specifications. According to the renders, Sony Xperia 5 Plus is said to sport a boxy design with metal to the sides and is said to flaunt a flat 6.6-inch OLED display. The display resolution and aspect ratio is known yet but considering the Xperia 5 sports an FHD+ 21:9 display, we can expect the Xperia 5 Plus to sport a 21:9 display as well. Furthermore, it is said to sport quad rear cameras and it is said to be a combination of triple rear cameras along with a time-of-flight (ToF) sensor. The front camera is said to be an 8MP shooter, but there is no information on the resolution of the rear cameras yet. Furthermore, it is said to come with a side-mounted fingerprint scanner, 3.5mm jack to the top, and feature dual front-firing speakers.
