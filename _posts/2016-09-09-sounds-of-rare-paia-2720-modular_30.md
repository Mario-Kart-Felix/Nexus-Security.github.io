> This video, via [Tinfoil Cat](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC1avKBqEnSkT6KRVDTi0MGA), explores the sounds of the rare **PAiA 2720 Modular Synthesizer**.
> 
> [PAiA Electronics](https://www.paia.com/), founded by **John Simonton** in 1967, pioneered synth DIY in an era where commercial modular synthesizers were as expensive as cars. The PAiA 2720 system was advertised in electronics magazines, and available as a collection of kits.
