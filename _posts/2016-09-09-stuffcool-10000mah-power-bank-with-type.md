![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/Stuffcool-1000mAh-Type-C-18W-power-bank.jpg) Stuffcool, the company known for its wide range of accessories like cases, screen protectors, power banks has today launched its new 10000mAh power bank in India. It is a compact power bank with USB Type-C port, Quick Charge 3.0 support, and more. Stuffcool 10000mAh power bank has a textured design, features a single USB-A port that is compatible with Qualcomm Quick Charge 3.0 (18W max), and also features a USB Type-C port with 18W Power Delivery to charge compatible devices 50-70% faster and the power bank itself. It is BIS approved, has a LED indicator that displays the functionality of the charger and the company has included a 3A Type C cable in the box to charge the power bank. It is priced at Rs. 1799 but is available at an introductory price of Rs. 1399. This product is available to purchase from Stuffcool's website and Amazon.in.
