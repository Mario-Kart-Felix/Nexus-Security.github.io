Feather Wing PMOD Adapter
-------------------------

By Tom Keddie:

> This is a simple FeatherWing to allow pmod modules to be used with feather boards. The connectors are compliant with the UART, SPI and I2C pmod standards. The two vertical connectors are spaced to allow a dual pmod to be inserted.
