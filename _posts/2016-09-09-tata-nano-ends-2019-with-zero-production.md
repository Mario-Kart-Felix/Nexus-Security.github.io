[Tata Nano ends 2019 with zero production](https://varindia.com/news/tata-nano-ends-2019-with-zero-production#.XhR6Va_mJ6I.blogger): In a report, Tata Motors is set to announce its plans to shelve the Tata Nano as it did not produce a single unit of its entry-level car in 2019 although it sold just one unit in February. According to a regulatory filing by the company, Tata Motors had zero production and sales of its Nano in December 2019 while it produced 82 units and sold 88 units in December 2018.
