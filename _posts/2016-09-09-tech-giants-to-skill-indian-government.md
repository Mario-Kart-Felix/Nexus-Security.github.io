[Tech giants to skill Indian government officials in AI, Cloud](https://varindia.com/news/tech-giants-to-skill-indian-government-officials-in-ai-cloud#.XZSEVWp-SNA.blogger): It has been reported that tech giants are all set to train government officials. The daunting task ahead is to train millions of government officials so that these disruptive technologies can be optimized for the benefit of over one billion-plus people.
