College students always have a lot going on. On any given day, you’ll find them juggling between numerous tasks like attending classes, tests, projects, assignments, and social activities.

So, while picking up a tech gift for them, it’s best to look for something that will fit in their fast-paced lifestyles and make their lives easier. Here are the best tech gifts for students you can choose from.

1\. [Google Nest Hub Max](https://store.google.com/us/product/google_nest_hub_max)
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[](https://store.google.com/us/product/google_nest_hub_max)

Google’s smart display, the [Nest Hub Max](https://store.google.com/us/product/google_nest_hub_max) can prove useful to a student in several ways. With built-in stereo speakers, the Nest Hub Max will ensure they never miss an alarm or appointment. You can ask the Google Assistant a wide range of questions and view the results on a wide 10-inch touchscreen.

In addition, the Nest Hub Max supports various other services. You can read step-by-step recipes and configure it to display a slideshow of your pictures. The Google Nest Hub Max can also keep an eye on your dorm room. Since it can function as a home security camera, you have the ability to check the camera’s live feed from your phone remotely.
