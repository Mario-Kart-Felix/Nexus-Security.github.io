Via a tweet by [@JohnKennedyMSFT](https://twitter.com/JohnKennedyMSFT/status/1186701828487970816), the source code for the original Altair BASIC – written by Bill Gates, Paul Allen and Monte Davidoff – is [on GitHub](https://github.com/option8/Altair-BASIC)!

> Micro-Soft Altair BASIC 3.2 (4K) – Annotated Disassembly
> 
> Copyright 1975, Bill Gates, Paul Allen, Monte Davidoff
> 
> Source: [http://altairbasic.org/](http://altairbasic.org/) compiled by Reuben Harris
> 
> Additional cleanup, relocation by Charles Mangin, March, 2019
