Whether it's to delete a few photos or share a bunch with another app, there are times when you need to select several pictures at once. This would usually be annoying since you'd have to tap every picture you wanted to select, but there's a much faster way to batch select. Open Photos and choose "Select" in the top-right corner. Now, just slide your finger left to right across the images of choice, and a blue checkmark will appear on each selected photo.
