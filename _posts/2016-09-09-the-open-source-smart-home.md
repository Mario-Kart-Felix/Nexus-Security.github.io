\[Tijmen Schep\] sends in his project, [Candle Smart Home, which is an exhibit of 12 smart home devices](https://www.candlesmarthome.com/) which are designed around the concepts of ownership, open source, and privacy.

The central controller runs on a Raspberry Pi which is running Mozilla’s new [smart home operating system](https://hacks.mozilla.org/2019/04/introducing-mozilla-webthings/).
