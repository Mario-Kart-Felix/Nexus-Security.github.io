There's a lot to love about iOS 13. Permanent Memoji stickers though? Not so much. Every time you go to use an emoji, you have to see the stickers, whether you want to or not. That is, until now. You see, iOS 13.3, currently in beta, introduces a new option that disables Memoji and Animoji stickers in the Emoji keyboard.
