If you're like me, you reorganize your home screen every few months. But instead of dragging the old icons and dropping them on that "Remove" button up top, there's a much faster, and quite frankly, more satisfying way to do it. To try it, after long-pressing the app icon, flick it up as if you're throwing it away. Swipe up quickly and let go, then the app will be removed from your home screen. This gesture is not only easier to perform, but reduces the risk of dropping your phone, especially when using larger devices.
