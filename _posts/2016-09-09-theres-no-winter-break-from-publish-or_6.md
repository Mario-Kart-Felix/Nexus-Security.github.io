Though many of us were able to take it easy the last couple of weeks and experiment with new levels of laziness, recent data shows that most scientists weren’t afforded the same leisure time.

Via [The New York Times](https://www.nytimes.com/2019/12/18/science/scientists-holiday-work.html)

> An analysis of submissions to two top journals showed that scientists in the U.S. were highly likely to be working during holidays.
