Great piece by [Thijs Biersteker](https://thijsbiersteker.com/pollutive-ends) via [Design You Trust](https://designyoutrust.com/2019/10/an-interactive-artwork-that-wants-us-to-focus-on-the-smallest-biggest-polluter-out-there-the-cigarette-butt/):

> 1 cigarette butt pollutes 500 Liters of water per day.
> 
> 5.7 Trillion cigarettes are smoked a year,  
> 67% of those are ending up in nature.

[Read more](https://thijsbiersteker.com/pollutive-ends) and see more on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=T5Hnm8cS-NM)
