This story is great fodder for a sci-fi movie! What kind of unforeseen repercussions could come from selling your likeness?

Via Vice:

> [Geomiq](https://geomiq.com/blog/public-appeal-applicants-needed-to-find-face-of-new-line-of-robots/), a London-based engineering company, says it’s looking for the perfect face for its new client, an undisclosed robot maker that’s developing a “virtual friend” for elderly people.
> 
> And they’re willing to pay someone £100,000—or around $127,840—to be that face, in perpetuity.
