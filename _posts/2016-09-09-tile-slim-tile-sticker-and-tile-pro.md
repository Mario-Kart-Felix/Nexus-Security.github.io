![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/Tile-Sticker-Tile-Slim-Tile-Pro-trackers.png) Tile, the world’s leading lost and found community has today launched its popular trackers — Tile Sticker, Tile Slim, and Tile Pro in India. These provide great functionality and convenience to help users find their belongings and these new devices are available in India at Amazon.in starting today. Tile Sticker is a small, adhesive-backed, waterproof finder that measures 27mm x 7.3mm in size, has a 46m (150ft) range and built-in 3-year battery. The company has partnered with 3M to design the adhesive for Sticker and it can be attached to most metal and plastic materials, including remote controls, cameras, and outdoor gear. Tile Slim is a credit card shaped tracker that measures 86mm x 54mm x 2.4mm in size, and fits into wallets and passport holders. It is waterproof, has a range of 61m (200ft) and built-in 3-year battery. Lastly, Tile Pro has a range of 122m (400ft) and loudest ring among others. It measures 42mm x 42mm x 6.5mm in size, is water-resistant, and packs a 1-Year Replaceable CR2032 battery.
