Dave Briccetti on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cDhqyT3ZN0g) demonstrates a tilt controlled music player. Coded in [CircuitPython](https://circuitpython.org/), it runs on an [Adafruit Circuit Playground Express board](https://www.adafruit.com/product/3333).

> I demonstrate and explain the code of a CircuitPython project where you tilt a Circuit Playground Express to play arpeggios in a circle of fourths. Y-axis tilt chooses the note. Buttons A and B advance forward and backward through the circle. The switch selects the type of arpeggio, either dominant seventh or blues.

The code is available here [https://github.com/dcbriccetti/Adafruit\_CircuitPython\_CircuitPlayground/blob/tilt-instrument/examples/advanced\_examples/circuitplayground\_tilting\_arpeggios.py](https://github.com/dcbriccetti/Adafruit_CircuitPython_CircuitPlayground/blob/tilt-instrument/examples/advanced_examples/circuitplayground_tilting_arpeggios.py)
