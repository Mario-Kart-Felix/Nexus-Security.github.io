One of the best additions offers the ability to turn off photo and video edits without undoing them, so you can preview how your shots look with or without an effect. How does it work? Let's say you're unhappy with the Vignette setting you tweaked for a photo or video and would prefer to disable the option altogether. Just tap the icon above the effect's slider, and the adjustment is disabled.
