![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Toreto-Bash-1024x415.jpg) Toreto, the brand known for its wide range of speakers, power banks, and other electronics launched several products last year in India. Now, the company has launched its first product for 2020 - Toreto Bash, a portable Bluetooth speaker with support for Bluetooth 5.0, hands-free calling, and more. Toreto Bash is compact and light-weight and fits in one's pocket easily. It is a 5W wireless speaker with support for Bluetooth 5.0 and True Wireless Connection. It packs a 1200mAh battery that promises 6 hours of playback time and a full charge takes 1.5 hours. The True Wireless Connection lets users connect two speakers at the same time via Bluetooth V5.0. Apart from this, it also has an in-built microphone with support for hands-free calling, FM support and the physical ports include an AUX port, TF Card Slot, and USB port. Pricing and Availability Toreto Bash comes in Black, Grey, and Red color options and is priced at Rs. 1799 in India. It will be available on popular e-commerce platforms and retail stores across the country.
