![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Toreto-Bloom-2-Bloom-3-Smartwatch.jpg) Toreto brand has today launched its new Bloom Series smartwatches in India — Toreto Bloom 2 and Bloom 3. This comes after the launch of other accessories from the brand like power banks, earphones, and more recently. Toreto Bloom 2 (TOR 82) & Bloom 3 (TOR 83) Toreto Bloom 2 and Bloom 3 have the functionality of a smartwatch cum fitness tracker and sports a 1.3-inch display with a single touch sensor wherein users can get all the information about their daily health and fitness activities with one touch on the screen. However, Bloom 3 comes with an improved screen sensor and both are powered by HS6220D CPU. It supports Bluetooth 4.0, compatible with iOS 8.0 or above and Android 4.4 or above. Both devices come with a training mode for cycling, trekking and running in tracks, etc and also supports smart notifications. Bloom 2 packs a 170mAh battery while the Bloom 3 packs a 150mAh battery and claims to deliver 1 week of battery life and 2 weeks standby time on a single charge.
