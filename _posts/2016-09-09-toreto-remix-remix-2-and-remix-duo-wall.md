![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/Toreto-Remix-Duo-1024x853.jpeg) Toreto has created a unique product that combines a Bluetooth speaker with a wall charger, called the Toreto Remix. The product comes in 3 varieties with up to dual 2.4Amp charging support and a 3W speaker built-in. The wall plug can connect to devices through Bluetooth 5.0 and has a 400mAh battery built in to power it 3W speakers. The Toreto Remix comes in 3 versions — Remix, Remix 2 and Remix Duo. All three versions have 2.4A fast charging support and can be connected to another charger plus via TWS technology. The wall plugs come only in white colour, comes with multi-circuit protection built-in and will retail for Rs. 1299, Rs. 1499 and Rs 1999 for the Remix, Remix 2 and Remix Duo respectively on Amazon.in.
