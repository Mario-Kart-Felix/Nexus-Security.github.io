![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Toreto-Torpods-1-1024x669.jpg) Toreto, a popular Indian audio manufacturer has launched its latest product, the Toreto Torpods. It is a pair of truly wireless earbuds that Toreto claims will have a better fit, better sound quality and longer battery life. The earbuds feature Bluetooth 5.0 Technology with Toreto's Auto Pairing Connectivity feature.  The earbuds have a snug stay-in design that the company promises will give all-day comfort for the user. The earbuds come with a magnetic charging case that will automatically charge the earbuds once its placed in. The 43 mAh battery on the earbuds will allow music playback for up to 4 hours with the 800mAh battery in the case providing another 3 full charges to bring the total listening time to 15 hours. The earbuds feature Bluetooth 5.0 and has separate microphones to allow the user to take calls. The Toreto Torpods is available in black colour only at a price tag of Rs. 4,499 (MRP) with one year warranty from the manufacturer. It can be purchased from their website for Rs. 3,599, but you can get it from Amazon.in for Rs. 2999.
