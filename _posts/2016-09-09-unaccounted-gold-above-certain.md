[Unaccounted gold above a certain threshold may account for heavy tax](https://varindia.com/news/unaccounted-gold-above-a-certain-threshold-may-account-for-heavy-tax#.XbwjvyBdmdA.blogger): To curb black money, the Narendra Modi government will soon announce an amnesty scheme for unaccounted gold. Individuals will be allowed to disclose unaccounted gold and beyond a certain threshold will have to pay taxes.
