[US investor to cut down Indian government bond holdings](https://varindia.com/news/us-investor-to-cut-down-indian-government-bond-holdings#.XiFtKuNvZR4.blogger): Tensions and protests arising in the wake of introduction of CAA and the revoking of Article 370 in Kashmir has led US based Western Asset Management, which manages $453 billion assets, to reduce its Indian government bond holdings. The investor feels that this could possibly impact economic outlook.
