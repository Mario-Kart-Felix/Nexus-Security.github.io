Folders are great for organizing apps and removing clutter from your home screen. But once they become overfull, it's not as easy to find and launch an app. If you have a jailbroken device, however, there's a tweak that will lessen the hassle of rummaging through folders and make it easier to open the apps they contain. David Goldman's Manila tweak lets you use either 3D Touch or Haptic Touch on app folders to open apps directly without having to open the folders they're in.
