Adafruit will not be shipping USPS orders Monday October 14, 2019 because of the federal holiday, Columbus Day.

Expedited USPS orders placed after 11am EST Friday October 11 will go out Tuesday October 15.

All UPS and DHL orders will go out as normal.

Learn more about [shipping](https://www.adafruit.com/shippinginfo/) and [holidays](https://www.adafruit.com/holiday)!
