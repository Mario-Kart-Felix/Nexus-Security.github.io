![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/Vivo-U20-India-launch-teaser-1024x615.jpg) Vivo launched the U10 smartphone, the first in the U20 series in India back in September. Now the company has confirmed U20, the second smartphone in the U series will be launched in the country on November 22nd on Amazon.in. The image shows a water-drop notch screen, triple rear cameras, gradient back and a rear-mounted fingerprint scanner. The company has also confirmed Snapdragon 675 for the phone, compared to Snapdragon 665 in the U10. This will be the third Vivo phone to launch in India with Snapdragon 675 after the Vivo V15 Pro and the latest V17 Pro. It has also confirmed up to 6GB of RAM for the phone, and UFS 2.1 storage, so there could be 4GB + 64GB and 6GB + 128GB versions. It will likely feature a Full HD+ LCD screen. We can expect more specifications of the phone in the coming days, and the price when it is launched next Friday.
