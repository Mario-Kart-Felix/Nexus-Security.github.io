![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Vivo-Y11-2019.png) Vivo has just announced its latest smartphone in Vietnam - Vivo Y11 (2019) and has identical design language to Vivo Y12 that was launched earlier this year in India. It comes with dual rear cameras, a large 5000mAh battery, Android 9 (Pie), and more. Vivo Y11 sports a 6.35-inch HD+ Halo FullView LCD display with a resolution of 720 × 1544 pixels, is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 439 processor paired with 3GB RAM with 32GB internal storage which can be expanded with a microSD card. It has a glass-like dual-tone design with a rear-mounted physical fingerprint scanner. The Y11 (2019) runs on Android 9 (Pie) on top of Funtouch OS 9.1. Regarding the optics, the Y11 (2019) sports a dual rear camera setup with a combination of 13MP (primary sensor with f/2.2 aperture) + 2MP(secondary sensor with f/2.4 aperture) and the front camera is an 8MP sensor with f/1.8 aperture. The cameras come with AI capabilities and support various photo modes like Professional, PDAF, Palm Shoot, Voice Capture, Time Lapse, Slow Motion, Animation, HDR, Panorama, Portrait, Watermark, AI Face Beauty, and Camera Filter.
