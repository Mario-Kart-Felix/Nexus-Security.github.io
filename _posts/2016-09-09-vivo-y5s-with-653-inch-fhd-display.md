![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/Vivo-Y5s.jpg) Vivo has announced Y5s, the company's latest mid-range smartphone in China. It has a 6.53-inch Full HD+ display with about 90.3% screen-to-body ratio, is powered by the latest MediaTek Helio P65 12nm SoC with 6GB of RAM, sports a 16MP primary sensor with f/1.78 aperture+ 8MP ultrawide sensor and an 2MP macro sensor, as well as a 16MP front-facing camera. In has a rear-mounted fingerprint scanner and packs a 5000mAh battery with support for Vivo’s 18W Dual Engine fast charging, but it has a micro USB port.
