![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/Vivo-Y91-1.jpg) Vivo launched the Y91 smartphone earlier this year in India starting at Rs. 10,990 and later launched the Y91i smartphone starting at Rs. 7990. Both smartphones got a price cut back in May this year and now again both Vivo Y91 and Y91i have received a price cut in India and will be available at revised prices online and offline stores as well. Vivo Y91 3GB RAM with 32GB storage variant will be available for Rs. 8490, against its current selling price of Rs. 8990 and this means it has received a Rs. 500 price cut. Vivo Y91i, on the other hand, 2GB RAM with 16GB storage variant will be available for Rs. 6990, against its current selling price of Rs. 7490 and this means it has received a Rs. 500 price cut as well. The revised prices will be reflected on e-commerce websites like Flipkart, Amazon.in soon and both the smartphones will be available at revised prices in offline stores as well.
