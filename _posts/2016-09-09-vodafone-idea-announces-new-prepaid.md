![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/Vodafone-Idea-Merger.jpg) Last month, Vodafone Idea, Airtel, and Jio announced prepaid tariff hikes that will be effective from this month. On similar lines, Vodafone Idea has now announced its new prepaid plans which will be available across India starting 3rd December 2019. The new prepaid plans from Vodafone Idea are categorized into Combo Vouchers, Unlimited Packs with 28 days validity, 84 days validity, 365 days validity, Unlimited Sachet plans, and First Recharge plans. Calls (Local, STD and on National Roaming) from Vodafone to Vodafone and Vodafone to Idea and vice versa are considered as on-net and the rest are off-net. Off-net calls after the free usage in the plan would be charged at 6p/min, similar to Jio, but the unlimited plans come with bundled FUP minutes without additional cost unlike Jio.
