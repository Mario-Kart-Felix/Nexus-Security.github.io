What’s better than an infinity mirror? A wearable infinity mirror that displays the time! From nolandoktor on [instructables](https://www.instructables.com/id/Vortex-Watch-an-Infinity-Mirror-Wristwatch/):

> It uses its RGB LEDs to indicate time by assigning hours, minutes, and seconds to Red, Green, and Blue lights respectively and overlapping these hues to create primary, secondary, and tertiary colors that represent the time.

[Read more](https://www.instructables.com/id/Vortex-Watch-an-Infinity-Mirror-Wristwatch/) and see on on [YouTube](https://youtu.be/t5Mm1kmOYa8)
