The weekend is the best for relaxing activities, including binge-watching some [Amazon Prime](https://cc.zdtc.app/v1/otc/05YcxvrJv7bM0tkN3uaIbHd?veneer=dynamic&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.primevideo.com%2F) flicks. We’ve rounded up the best movies, TV shows, and documentaries that you can stream on [Amazon Prime](https://www.geek.com/television/heres-everything-coming-to-amazon-prime-video-in-november-2019-1808225/) from **November 16 to November 17.** Democratic fights, swordmasters, and magical creatures will keep you chilling on the couch from today to Sunday.

  

_Ray Donovan_
-------------

  

  

Ray Donovan is the ultimate “fixer” for celebrities, but sometimes, he can’t make their troubles disappear. Season 7 of _Ray Donovan_ follows the protagonist, as he has to deal with the NYC mayor, an undetermined NYPD officer, and new clients.

  

[**Stream it Here**](https://www.amazon.com/gp/video/detail/B07YXDXZ12/ref=atv_dp)

  

_Blade of the Immortal_
-----------------------

  

_Blade of the Immortal_ is one of the best anime series to watch. The Slayer of Hundreds is asked to be the bodyguard of a young girl who wants to seek revenge against a group of swordmasters. The battle between mortals and immortals begins.

  

[**Stream it Here**](https://www.amazon.com/gp/video/detail/B07YTN1SH8)

  

_The Man in the High Castle _
-----------------------------

  

  

_The Man in the High Castle_ is in its fourth and last season. Set in an alternate world, the series follows the power of the Nazis, as they try to take over the world. Juliana Crain gets to see our world, where America wins World War II, and now, she’s going to try to set history straight.

  

[**Stream it Here**](https://www.amazon.com/gp/video/detail/B07T8K8V2D/ref=atv_dp)

  

_The Christmas Lodge_
---------------------

  

_The Christmas Lodge_ is a heartwarming holiday movie about restoring a family’s legacy. Mary, who’s trying to enjoy her mountain retreat, aims to rebuild her run-down family lodge with guidance from her grandparents, including one that’s not alive. (Get ready for the waterworks.)

  

[**Stream it Here**](https://www.amazon.com/Christmas-Lodge-Erin-Karpluk/dp/B07KLR41TS)

  

_The Secret Life of Walter Mitty_
---------------------------------

  

  

A photo is missing, but Walter Mitty’s head is still in the clouds and he wants to finish his friend’s work. The _Secret Life of Walter Mitty_ tells the story of a magazine worker who escapes from reality to deal with internal pain. When his job is in danger, he embarks on the global journey of his dreams to put the pieces back together.

  

[**Stream it Here**](https://www.amazon.com/gp/video/detail/B00ISK88B2)

  

_Grimm_
-------

  

Nick Burkhardt, a Portland detective, has to protect humans from magical creatures in the fantasy series _Grimm_. As a descendant from a line of warriors, Burkhardt comes in close contact with werewolves, zombies, and other spooky beings in this show that exist in modern society.

  

[**Stream it Here**](https://www.amazon.com/gp/video/detail/B01MRXURG)
