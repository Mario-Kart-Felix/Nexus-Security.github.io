[WhatsApp to end its support for Windows Phone from today](https://varindia.com/news/whatsapp-to-end-its-support-for-windows-phone-from-today#.Xgs0btJTmvI.blogger): Starting from today, i.e. December 31st, WhatsApp is officially ending its support for the Windows Phone platform managed by Microsoft. The popular messaging application has decided to withdraw support from all Windows Phone versions from today.
