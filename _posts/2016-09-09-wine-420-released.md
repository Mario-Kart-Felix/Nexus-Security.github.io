News
====

[Wine 4.20 Released](https://www.winehq.org/news/2019111501)

November 15, 2019

The Wine development release 4.20 is now available.

[What's new](https://www.winehq.org/announce/4.20) in this release:

*   New version of the Mono engine with an FNA update.
*   Code persistence in VBScript and JScript.
*   Vulkan spec updates.
*   Improved support for LLVM MinGW.
*   Various bug fixes.

The source is [available now](https://dl.winehq.org/wine/source/4.x/wine-4.20.tar.xz). Binary packages are in the process of being built, and will appear soon at their respective [download locations](https://www.winehq.org/download).
