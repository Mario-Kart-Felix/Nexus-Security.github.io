![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/MIUI-11-1024x517.jpg) Xiaomi announced MIUI 11 Global along with Redmi Note 8 and Redmi Note 8 Pro in India last month, and also shared the 4-phased rollout schedule for various devices. The company has already rolled out MIUI 11 Global Stable for the first phase of devices including Redmi K20 Pro, Redmi K20, POCO F1, Redmi Note 7 Pro, Redmi 7, Redmi Y3 and MIUI 11 Global Stable Beta testing for the second phase of devices is ongoing. The company has a tradition of involving users in MIUI testing for their devices and in case you want to explore MIUI 11 Global Stable on Redmi Note 8, Redmi 8 or Redmi 8A, there is good news for you as Xiaomi is looking for MIUI Stable ROM testers for the third phase of devices, you can apply here to be a part of the MIUI testing team in case you’re interested.
