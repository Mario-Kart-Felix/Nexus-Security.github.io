![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/MIUI-11-1024x517.jpg) Xiaomi announced MIUI 11 Global earlier this week along with Redmi Note 8 and Redmi Note 8 Pro in India, and also shared the 4-phased rollout schedule for various devices. As per the official schedule, the first batch of devices including POCO F1, Redmi K20 Pro, Redmi K20, and Redmi Note 7 Pro will receive MIUI 11 Stable update starting 22nd October. The company has a tradition of involving users in MIUI testing for their devices and in case you want to explore MIUI 11 Global Stable before everyone else, there is good news for you as Xiaomi is looking for MIUI Stable ROM testers for various devices, you can apply here to be a part of the MIUI testing team in case you’re interested. As per the recruitment thread posted on Mi Community, the company is looking for MIUI 11 Global Stable Beta testing for the following devices, Redmi K20 Pro Redmi K20 POCO F1 Redmi Note 7 Pro Redmi Note 7S Redmi Note 7  Redmi Y3 Redmi 7 The recruitment is open to all users with limited slots, users are also supposed to install an instant messenger Telegram and join the telegram group to discuss bugs with other testers and ...
