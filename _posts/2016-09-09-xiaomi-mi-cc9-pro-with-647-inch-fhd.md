![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Xiaomi-CC9-Pro.jpg)Yesterday Xiaomi confirmed that it will introduce the Mi CC9 Pro smartphone in China on November 5th. Today the phone has been certified by TENAA with the model number M1910F4E revealing the complete specifications. The company has also posted a new teaser, which shows the gradient glass back in Green color, and there is a Xiaomi branding. You can also see the 3.5mm audio jack on the bottom along with the USB Type-C port and a loudspeaker grill.
