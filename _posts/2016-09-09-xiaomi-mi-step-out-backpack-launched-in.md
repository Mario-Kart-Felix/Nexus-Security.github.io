![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/Mi-Step-Out-Backpack.jpg) Xiaomi launched Mi Business Casual Backpack for office goers earlier this year. Today it has launched its new Mi Step Out Backpack with 12 liter storage and waterproof coating. It also comes in a range of colors and comes with 2 zipper pockets that can house a notebook, gym gear, travel essentials and more. Mi Step Out Backpack 12 liters capacity with 2 zipper pockets Multi-coated, water-repellant 600D polyester fabric design keeps your belongings safe whether you’re on a short excursion or bustling through a commute. Fully equipped with a water repellent exterior, contra zippers, reflective pullers, and adjustable shoulder straps Weighing just 200g, this backpack is easy to carry The Xiaomi Mi Step Out Backpack comes in Black, Dark Blue, Royal Blue and Red colors, is priced at Rs. 249, is available from mi.com and is back by 6 months warranty.
