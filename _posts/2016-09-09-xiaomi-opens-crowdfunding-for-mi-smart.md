![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Mi-Smart-Bedside-Lamp-2.jpg) Xiaomi today opened crowd funding for its new Mi Smart Bedside Lamp 2 in India. It is a 12W lamp with 400 Lumens output, and you can control the lamp through the app, and choose from 16 million colors. It works with Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant as well as Apple HomeKit. Xiaomi says that it will last for 11 years with an average use of 6 hours per day.
