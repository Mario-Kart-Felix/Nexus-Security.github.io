![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Xiaomi-2.5-lakh-Mi-TV-1024x512.jpg) Diwali festive sale kicked off recently across major e-commerce platforms like Flipkart, Amazon.in with offers from various smartphone brands including Xiaomi, Realme, Samsung, OPPO, Vivo, and more. Today, Xiaomi has revealed that it has achieved a record-breaking sale of over 2,50,000 or 2.5 lakh Mi TVs in the first few days across Diwali With Mi on mi.com, The Big Billion Days on Flipkart, and Great Indian Shopping Festival on Amazon. Xiaomi has sold over 43 Mi TVs per minute and the company says that the start of festive season sale also saw Mi TVs achieving bestseller status across partner platforms, Amazon.in and Flipkart. According to the recordings during various intervals on Amazon and Flipkart, Top Two out of five best-selling Smart TVs are Mi TVs on Amazon Mi TV 4C PRO (32) and Mi TV 4A PRO 43 are #1 and #2 respectively on Amazon Mi TV is the highest-selling TV on Amazon and Flipkart Mi TV is the #1 TV on Flipkart in terms of value Eshwar Nilakanta, Category Lead - MiTV, Xiaomi India said: Our Smart TV business was born in 2018 and it has flourished well in India.
