![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/MIUI-12.jpg) Xiaomi announced the latest iteration of its own proprietary Android skin MIUI, dubbed MIUI 11 along with Mi 9 Pro 5G, Mi MIX Alpha 5G, and Mi AirDots Pro 2 at an event in China at the end of September, and it was roll out to range of devices starting from October. Today the company has started teasing the MIUI 12, the next major update with a new image. This shows a blue colored gradient on the number '12' which hints at even more visual changes. The MIUI 11 brought new optimized modern UI design focused on content viewing and also received two new Red Dot design awards for the new visual design and sound effects.  The company doesn't reveal what the new MIUI 12 update will bring since it is still in early stages of development, but it expected to feature improved animation, full screen gesture improvements for its upcoming curved screen products, new fonts, sound effects and more. This year coincides with the 10th anniversary of the founding of Xiaomi.
