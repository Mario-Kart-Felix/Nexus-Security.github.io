![](https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/Xiaomi-Watch-Color-2-1024x616.jpg) Xiaomi launched its Wear OS-based Mi Watch with AMOLED display, eSIM support last month. Following that, Xiaomi's Mijia or Mi Ecosystem has now announced its new 'Watch Color' with a circular display, various color strap options, and more. Mijia has just posted a teaser image of the Watch Color smartwatch on Weibo and it doesn't specify the specifications. The teaser image reveals a circular display, 1540 customizable color combinations, and the image also mentions "long battery life". It also seems to be waterproof, according to the teaser images. \[video width="1280" height="720" mp4="https://images.fonearena.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/Xiaomi-Watch-Color.mp4"\]\[/video\] The display is said to be 1.39-inch in size with a resolution of 454 x 454 resolution and it resembles Amazfit GTR which was launched back in September this year. The Watch Color will go on sale in China on January 3rd, 2020, and we should know the price and complete specifications. Source
