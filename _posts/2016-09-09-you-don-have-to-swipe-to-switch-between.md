Swiping between pages on your iPhone's home screen feels very natural, but surprisingly, Apple has another way to switch between screens, and it's been staring us in the face this whole time. See the tiny dots on the bottom of your iPhone's home screen and app folders? Besides showing you what page you're currently on, you can also tap on those to switch pages without having to swipe. It's a handy alternative to have if swiping isn't ideal due to discomfort from joint pain, or if you simply feel the method is just more intuitive for you.
